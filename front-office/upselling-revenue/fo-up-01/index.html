Identification

  • ID FO-UP-01
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Upselling & Revenue Optimisation
  • Process Pre-Arrival Upsell Campaign Management
  • Trigger The initiation of this process is triggered by the confirmation of a reservation in the Oracle OPERA Cloud system.
  • Outcome Successful completion produces an increased revenue through targeted upselling offers to guests before their arrival, enhancing guest satisfaction and loyalty.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to generate the upsell opportunity report
  • ▸ Less than 45 minutes to segment guests
  • ▸ Less than 60 minutes to create the upsell campaign plan
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours to send pre-arrival emails
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour to generate the campaign performance report
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours to finalize campaign metrics

Description

A structured process document for managing a pre-arrival upsell campaign at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate identification of upsell opportunities leading to missed revenue potential
  • ▸ Poor segmentation resulting in ineffective targeted marketing
  • ▸ Low engagement rates from pre-arrival emails due to unpersonalized content
  • ▸ Difficulty in accurately measuring the impact of upsell campaigns
  • ▸ Failure to maintain up-to-date guest information in the CRM
