ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-UP-07
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Upselling & Revenue Optimisation
  • Process Ancillary Revenue Reporting
  • Trigger Monthly end of business cycle
  • Outcome A comprehensive report detailing ancillary revenue sources, trends, and opportunities for improvement is generated and shared with the Revenue Management team and upper management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data extraction within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Data validation completed in less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Trend analysis report generated in under 4 hours
  • ▸ Revenue optimization strategy document finalized in 6 hours or less
  • ▸ Report distribution log created within 1 hour

Description

The process involves generating and reporting ancillary revenue data from various hotel departments to optimize upselling strategies and improve overall financial performance.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in process initiation due to system issues
  • ▸ Data extraction errors leading to incomplete reports
  • ▸ Integration problems between Oracle OPERA Cloud and Marriott Bonvoy CRM
  • ▸ Misinterpretation of data trends affecting upselling strategies
  • ▸ Failed distribution attempts causing communication gaps with stakeholders
