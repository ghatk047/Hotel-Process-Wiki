Description

The process aims to optimize late check-out and early check-in revenue management at a Marriott full-service hotel by leveraging Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate room status updates leading to overbooking
  • ▸ Handling different payment methods securely and efficiently
  • ▸ Balancing short-term revenue gains with long-term planning
