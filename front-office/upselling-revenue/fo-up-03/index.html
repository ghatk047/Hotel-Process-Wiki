KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per guest for upsell conversations
  • ▸ Upsell success rate of at least 20%
  • ▸ Daily revenue report generated within 10 minutes
  • ▸ Guest profile updates completed in less than 2 minutes

Description

A structured process document for package and add-on selling in Marriott full-service hotels using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data entry leading to incorrect upsell recommendations
  • ▸ Guest dissatisfaction due to inappropriate upselling techniques
  • ▸ System downtime affecting the ability to track and manage upsells
  • ▸ Difficulty in maintaining a natural conversation flow during upselling
