ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-CI-07
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Check-In & Check-Out Operations
  • Process No-Show & Cancellation Processing
  • Trigger A reservation is marked as a no-show or cancelled by the guest or system.
  • Outcome The hotel successfully processes the no-show or cancellation, updating systems, notifying relevant parties, and adjusting resources accordingly.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to identify no-show/cancellation
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to update reservation status
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to notify Revenue Management
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to generate report

Description

This process outlines how a Marriott full-service hotel handles no-shows and cancellations, ensuring guest satisfaction and efficient resource management.

Risks

  • ▸ Manual entry errors leading to incorrect updates
  • ▸ System downtime affecting real-time data processing
  • ▸ Overbooking due to inaccurate room availability adjustments
  • ▸ Data synchronization issues between PMS and CRM
  • ▸ Resistance to change in implementing new pricing strategies
