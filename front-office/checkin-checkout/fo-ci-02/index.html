Identification

  • ID FO-CI-02
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Check-In & Check-Out Operations
  • Process Guest Check-In & Room Assignment
  • Trigger Guest arrival notification via reservation system or direct walk-in
  • Outcome Successful check-in with assigned room, confirmed reservations, and updated guest records in the Oracle OPERA Cloud system.

KPIs

  • ▸ Room assignment within 2 minutes (95% of the time)
  • ▸ Payment collection within 5 minutes (100% of the time)
  • ▸ Check-in confirmation within 3 minutes (98% of the time)
  • ▸ Housekeeping room readiness within 10 minutes (99% of the time)

Description

The FO-CI-02 process involves checking in guests and assigning rooms at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. This ensures smooth operations and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting reservation and check-in processes
  • ▸ Incorrect room assignments due to system errors
  • ▸ Payment processing delays leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Lost or damaged keys impacting guest experience
  • ▸ Inaccurate check-in information causing confusion
