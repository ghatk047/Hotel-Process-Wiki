  • Trigger Receiving a group reservation request in Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS
  • Outcome Successful completion of the group check-in process, ensuring all guests are checked into their assigned rooms on time with minimal disruption.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per reservation
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per group for room assignment
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per guest for check-in
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per group for feedback collection

Description

The Group Check-In Coordination process ensures a smooth and efficient check-in experience for large groups at full-service Marriott hotels. This process involves coordinating with various departments to manage reservations, room assignments, and guest communications.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate or incomplete reservation details leading to miscommunication
  • ▸ Room availability issues during high-occupancy periods
  • ▸ Lost or misplaced identification documents affecting check-in efficiency
  • ▸ Guest dissatisfaction due to poor room preparation or service quality
  • ▸ Delayed feedback collection impacting follow-up actions
