  • Trigger Detection of an overbooked status in the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS or identification of a no-show by a guest.
  • Outcome Successful management of overbooking and walk scenarios, ensuring minimal disruption to guests and maintaining hotel occupancy levels.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 min response time to overbooked status detection
  • ▸ Less than 2 min notification time from Revenue Manager to Front Desk
  • ▸ Less than 4 min to generate room availability report
  • ▸ Less than 30 min to contact no-show guests

Description

The process for managing overbooking and walk management at a Marriott full-service hotel involves monitoring reservations, notifying guests of potential changes, and adjusting room assignments to ensure guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ System integration issues affecting real-time monitoring of reservations
  • ▸ Communication delays between departments impacting the speed of response
  • ▸ Complex room configurations complicating the reassignment process
  • ▸ High volume of walk-ins overwhelming the front desk
  • ▸ Balancing staffing needs with varying guest volumes being challenging
