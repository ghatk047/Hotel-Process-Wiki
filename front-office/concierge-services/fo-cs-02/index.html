ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-CS-02
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Concierge & Guest Services
  • Process Transportation & Transfer Arrangement
  • Trigger A guest requests transportation services, such as airport transfers or car rentals, either through the front desk, concierge, or online via Marriott Bonvoy CRM.
  • Outcome Successful completion of this process results in timely and accurate transportation arrangements that meet the guest's needs, with all relevant parties notified and systems updated accordingly.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for receiving and logging transportation requests
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for identifying available transportation options
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for booking transportation services
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for updating Oracle OPERA Cloud with booking details

Description

This process outlines how a Marriott full-service hotel handles transportation and transfer arrangements for guests. It ensures smooth coordination between various departments and systems to provide efficient and personalized service.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate real-time availability data across multiple systems
  • ▸ Handling last-minute requests with limited availability
  • ▸ Integrating multiple booking systems for seamless transactions
  • ▸ Ensuring data consistency across systems
  • ▸ Handling unexpected delays or cancellations
