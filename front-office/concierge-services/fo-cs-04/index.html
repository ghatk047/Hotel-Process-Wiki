  • Trigger Guest arrival and check-in
  • Outcome Properly managed and stored luggage for guests, ensuring timely retrieval upon checkout or request.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per piece of luggage at check-in and retrieval
  • ▸ Continuous monitoring of luggage storage area
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per special request handling
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per PMS record update

Description

This process outlines the handling and storage of luggage at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. It ensures efficient management of guest luggage from check-in to departure.

Risks

  • ▸ System connectivity issues affecting real-time updates
  • ▸ Overcrowded storage areas leading to inefficiencies
  • ▸ Complex or unusual requests from guests
  • ▸ Late checkouts causing delays in luggage preparation
