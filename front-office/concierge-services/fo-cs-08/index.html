Identification

  • ID FO-CS-08
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Concierge & Guest Services
  • Process Special Occasion & Surprise Arrangement
  • Trigger Receptionist receives a request for a special occasion or surprise arrangement from a guest or external party.
  • Outcome Successful execution of the special occasion or surprise arrangement, with all details communicated to relevant parties and documented in the system.

KPIs

  • ▸ Request logged in Oracle OPERA Cloud within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Vendor coordination completed within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Guest notification sent within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Final plan approved within 10 minutes
  • ▸ Post-event review and documentation completed within 24 hours

Description

A process to handle special occasion and surprise arrangements at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring seamless coordination between departments and systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Complex requests overwhelming Front Desk Agents
  • ▸ Vendor availability issues leading to event delays
  • ▸ Communication breakdowns between departments
  • ▸ Technical system failures affecting coordination
  • ▸ Guest dissatisfaction due to unexpected changes in arrangements
