ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-CS-01
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Concierge & Guest Services
  • Process Concierge Request Fulfilment
  • Trigger Guest submits a concierge request through various channels (e.g., phone, email, in-room device).
  • Outcome Successful completion of the guest's concierge request with timely communication and satisfaction.

KPIs

  • ▸ Response time within 30 seconds
  • ▸ Assignment within 1 minute
  • ▸ Confirmation within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Completion within 1 hour for standard tasks
  • ▸ Notification within 10 minutes of completion
  • ▸ Status update within 5 minutes

Description

This process outlines how a Marriott full-service hotel fulfills concierge requests using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Urgent requests overwhelming the concierge team.
  • ▸ Miscommunication or misunderstanding of task requirements.
  • ▸ Unexpected delays or complications during task execution.
  • ▸ Inaccurate documentation leading to process inefficiencies.
  • ▸ Guest dissatisfaction due to delayed or incorrect notifications.
