Identification

  • Process Restaurant Reservation & Ticketing
  • Trigger Guest requests a reservation for a restaurant dining experience
  • Outcome Successful creation and confirmation of the restaurant reservation and ticketing process, with guest notified via preferred communication channel.

KPIs

  • ▸ < 3 min for receiving and processing reservation requests
  • ▸ < 1 min for verifying availability and generating tickets
  • ▸ < 2 min for sending confirmation notifications
  • ▸ < 5 min for monitoring reservation status

Description

Process for handling restaurant reservations and ticketing at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Conflicting reservation requests leading to overbooking
  • ▸ Real-time availability checks failing due to system issues
  • ▸ Undelivered or misdirected confirmation notifications
  • ▸ Last-minute changes causing operational disruptions
  • ▸ Incorrect finalization of reservations leading to guest dissatisfaction
