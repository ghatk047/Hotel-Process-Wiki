Identification

  • ID FO-CS-07
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Concierge & Guest Services
  • Process Guest Information & Local Knowledge
  • Trigger Guest arrival or request for local information
  • Outcome Guest receives relevant and up-to-date information about the hotel, local attractions, dining options, and other services.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for receiving and acknowledging guest requests
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute for querying the Marriott Bonvoy CRM
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes for accessing local knowledge databases
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for compiling information packages

Description

This process involves gathering and providing guest information and local knowledge at a Marriott full-service hotel. It ensures that guests receive accurate and timely information to enhance their stay.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate or incomplete data in the Marriott Bonvoy CRM
  • ▸ Database connectivity issues with Knowcross HotSOS
  • ▸ Resistance to change during process improvements
  • ▸ Handling unexpected guest requests or language barriers
  • ▸ Data entry errors when updating guest records
