ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-CS-05
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Concierge & Guest Services
  • Process Wake-Up Call & Guest Communications
  • Trigger Guest requests a wake-up call or communication service through various channels such as phone, online booking platform, or in-person request.
  • Outcome Successful completion ensures the guest receives their requested wake-up call or communication at the specified time and content.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to log request
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to verify guest details
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to schedule wake-up call
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to confirm with guest
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to prepare communication content
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to send wake-up call

Description

This process involves initiating and managing wake-up calls and guest communications at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other relevant systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Multiple requests overwhelming the front desk staff
  • ▸ Incorrect or incomplete guest data leading to errors
  • ▸ Conflicting schedules causing missed wake-up calls
  • ▸ Technical issues with the wake-up call system affecting delivery
  • ▸ Data entry errors in Oracle OPERA Cloud and CRM
