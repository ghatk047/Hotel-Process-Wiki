KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to implement the solution

Description

This process outlines how to handle guest complaints and achieve service recovery at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Delayed complaint resolution leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Inaccurate root cause analysis affecting solution effectiveness
  • ▸ Difficulty in obtaining management approval for solutions
  • ▸ Poor execution of solutions resulting in unresolved issues
  • ▸ Incomplete documentation and follow-up processes
