Identification

  • ID FO-SP-07
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Spa & Wellness Operations
  • Process Spa Cleanliness & Equipment Maintenance
  • Trigger Daily check-in of guests, end-of-day reports from Spa Attendants, and scheduled maintenance alerts from Oracle OPERA Cloud.
  • Outcome Spa areas are clean, well-maintained, and safe for use by all guests, with equipment functioning optimally to provide a seamless spa experience.

KPIs

  • ▸ Daily cleanliness check completion time: Less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Equipment maintenance log review time: Less than 15 minutes
  • ▸ Task assignment accuracy rate: 95% or higher
  • ▸ Spa equipment usage tracking accuracy: 98% or higher

Description

This process ensures that spa cleanliness and equipment maintenance are consistently high at a Marriott full-service hotel, enhancing guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent adherence to cleaning schedules leading to unsanitary conditions.
  • ▸ Overlooking critical equipment maintenance needs resulting in operational downtime.
  • ▸ Poor execution of cleaning protocols affecting guest satisfaction.
  • ▸ Delayed scheduling of equipment maintenance causing unexpected breakdowns.
