ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-SP-06
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Spa & Wellness Operations
  • Process Spa Membership & Package Management
  • Trigger A guest inquires about spa membership options or requests a package deal.
  • Outcome Successful management of spa memberships and packages, ensuring seamless transactions and guest satisfaction.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 1 min for identifying guest inquiry and confirming selection
  • ▸ Less than 3 min for processing payment and updating membership/package in PMS
  • ▸ Less than 5 min for consulting spa menu options
  • ▸ Daily monitoring of membership/package usage

Description

A process to manage spa memberships and packages at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Handling multiple inquiries simultaneously leading to operational delays
  • ▸ Payment issues or cancellations affecting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Ensuring accurate data entry and tracking usage across multiple systems
  • ▸ Dealing with renewals and expirations, especially for long-term memberships
  • ▸ Ensuring email delivery and content accuracy for confirmation emails
