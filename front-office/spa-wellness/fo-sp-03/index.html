  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services

  • Process Spa Reception & Guest Journey
  • Trigger Guest arrives at the spa for their scheduled appointment
  • Outcome Successful completion of the spa reception and guest journey with high satisfaction levels and accurate tracking of services provided.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per guest for check-in confirmation
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute per guest for spa arrival notification
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per guest for service selection
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per guest for post-spa feedback collection

Description

This process outlines the Spa Reception & Guest Journey at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring seamless guest experience from reservation to post-spa service.

Risks

  • ▸ Data entry errors in Oracle OPERA Cloud leading to incorrect reservations
  • ▸ Delayed communication between spa reception and therapists affecting guest experience
  • ▸ Handling of special dietary requirements during service selection
