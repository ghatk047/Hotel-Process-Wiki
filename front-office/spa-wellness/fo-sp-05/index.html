  • Trigger A request from the Spa Manager to schedule therapists based on demand forecasts and availability.
  • Outcome Successful scheduling of therapists, optimized utilization, and accurate tracking of appointments and performance metrics.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to update demand forecast
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to generate availability report
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to allocate therapists
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per appointment confirmation
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to generate performance evaluation report

Description

This process outlines how a full-service Marriott hotel schedules and utilizes therapists for spa and wellness services. It ensures efficient resource allocation and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate demand forecasts leading to over or underutilization of therapists.
  • ▸ Manual tracking of therapist schedules leading to errors.
  • ▸ Difficulty in balancing therapist workload across different services.
