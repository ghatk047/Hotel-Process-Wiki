KPIs

  • ▸ Data extraction time: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Revenue analysis time: Less than 3 hours
  • ▸ KPI calculation accuracy: 95%
  • ▸ Report preparation time: Less than 4 hours
  • ▸ Stakeholder distribution time: Less than 1 hour

Description

This process involves generating and reporting on spa revenue and key performance indicators (KPIs) at a Marriott full-service hotel. The goal is to ensure accurate data collection, analysis, and reporting to support business decisions and improve guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Data extraction errors leading to inaccurate reports
  • ▸ Complex data analysis resulting in delayed insights
  • ▸ Inconsistent stakeholder communication affecting report distribution
  • ▸ Operational changes not aligning with business goals
  • ▸ Failure to schedule the next reporting cycle timely
