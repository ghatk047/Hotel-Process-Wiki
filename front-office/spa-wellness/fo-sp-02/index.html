Identification

  • ID FO-SP-02
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Spa & Wellness Operations
  • Process Spa Treatment Delivery & Standards
  • Trigger A guest books a spa treatment through the reservation system or directly at the front desk.
  • Outcome Successful completion of the spa treatment with high guest satisfaction and accurate record-keeping in the PMS.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for receiving spa reservation
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for assigning spa treatment room
  • ▸ Treatment duration as per reservation
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for updating spa treatment records
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for processing spa payment

Description

This process outlines the delivery and standards for spa treatments at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Room availability conflicts during peak times
  • ▸ Stock management issues for spa products
  • ▸ Handling no-shows or late arrivals
  • ▸ Data entry errors in PMS
  • ▸ Maintaining guest loyalty and feedback collection
