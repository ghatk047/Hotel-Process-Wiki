ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FO-NS-04
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Night Audit & Front Desk Operations
  • Process Rate & Package Posting
  • Trigger Initiation by a Revenue Manager or Front Desk Agent upon receiving new rate or package information from external sources such as travel agencies, online platforms, or internal marketing teams.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in updated rates and packages being posted in Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS, IDeaS G3 RMS, SynXis CRS, and other relevant systems, ensuring that all guests receive accurate pricing information during the reservation process.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per rate review
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per data entry in Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per system sync
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per website verification

Description

The process of posting rates and packages in a full-service Marriott hotel involves ensuring that all pricing information is accurately entered into the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other relevant systems to maintain consistency across the property's operations.

Risks

  • ▸ Data discrepancies between systems affecting reservations
  • ▸ Integration issues leading to outdated pricing information
  • ▸ Communication delays causing confusion among staff
  • ▸ Lost or misplaced documents impacting process efficiency
  • ▸ System downtime disrupting data entry operations
