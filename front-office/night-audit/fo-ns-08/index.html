Identification

  • ID FO-NS-08
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Night Audit & Front Desk Operations
  • Process End-of-Day Reporting
  • Trigger End of business day (usually 10:00 PM local time)
  • Outcome Successful completion produces accurate end-of-day reports, updated financial records, and compliance with Marriott's operational standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Night Audit initiation within less than 2 min
  • ▸ Revenue verification completed in less than 5 min
  • ▸ Room status updates processed in less than 10 min
  • ▸ Guest satisfaction report generated in less than 15 min
  • ▸ End-of-day reports approved within less than 20 min

Description

The End-of-Day Reporting process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves closing out daily operations and generating reports to ensure accurate financial records and guest satisfaction metrics.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting night audit initiation and closure
  • ▸ Data discrepancies leading to inaccurate financial records
  • ▸ Employee scheduling conflicts impacting next day's operations
  • ▸ Insufficient storage or backup issues causing data loss
  • ▸ Resource constraints hindering process improvement efforts
