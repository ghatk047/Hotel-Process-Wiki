Description

The Guest Folio Management process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves managing guest billing and ensuring accurate financial transactions during their stay.

Risks

  • ▸ Data synchronization issues between Oracle OPERA Cloud and IDeaS G3 RMS
  • ▸ Manual entry errors leading to incorrect charges or refunds
  • ▸ Handling of declined payments causing delays in check-out process
