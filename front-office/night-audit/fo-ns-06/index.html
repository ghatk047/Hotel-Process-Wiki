Identification

  • ID FO-NS-06
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Night Audit & Front Desk Operations
  • Process Foreign Currency Exchange
  • Trigger A guest requests to exchange foreign currency at the front desk.
  • Outcome Successful completion of the currency exchange transaction, with accurate conversion rates applied, and all relevant records updated in the PMS system.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to provide the exchange rate quote
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to verify guest information
  • ▸ Less than 30 seconds to select the exchange rate
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to process payment
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to dispense foreign currency

Description

The Foreign Currency Exchange process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves handling currency exchange requests from guests, ensuring accurate transactions, and maintaining compliance with financial policies.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect or incomplete guest information leading to failed transactions
  • ▸ Outdated or inaccurate exchange rates affecting financial accuracy
  • ▸ Insufficient foreign currency stock causing transaction delays
  • ▸ Data entry errors in the PMS system resulting in incorrect records
  • ▸ Printing issues preventing the issuance of exchange receipts
