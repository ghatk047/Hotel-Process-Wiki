Identification

  • ID FO-NS-02
  • Domain Front Office & Guest Services
  • Group Night Audit & Front Desk Operations
  • Process Daily Revenue Balancing
  • Trigger The process is initiated by the end of each business day when all transactions are finalized.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in an accurate nightly revenue report, updated guest accounts, and a balanced ledger for financial reporting purposes.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for Oracle OPERA Cloud login
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for Night Audit report generation
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for discrepancy review
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per discrepancy adjustment

Description

Daily Revenue Balancing is a critical process at Marriott full-service hotels to ensure accurate financial reconciliation and reporting. This process involves verifying room revenue, adjusting discrepancies, and preparing reports for management.

Risks

  • ▸ System connectivity issues with IDeaS G3 RMS
  • ▸ Errors in adjusting transactions in Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • ▸ Incomplete or inaccurate documentation of adjustments
