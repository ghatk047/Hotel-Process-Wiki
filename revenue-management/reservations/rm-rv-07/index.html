ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID RM-RV-07
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Reservation Quality Auditing
  • Trigger Scheduled weekly audit by Revenue Management team or upon detection of reservation discrepancies.
  • Outcome Successful completion produces an audited list of reservations with any identified issues corrected, ensuring data accuracy and compliance with Marriott's standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per reservation for initiating the audit
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per reservation for reviewing reservations
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes per reservation for identifying issues
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per reservation for compiling the audit report

Description

The Reservation Quality Auditing process ensures that reservations at a Marriott full-service hotel are accurate and meet high standards of quality. This process is crucial for maintaining customer satisfaction and optimizing revenue management.

Risks

  • ▸ Data entry errors leading to incorrect reservations
  • ▸ Communication breakdowns between teams affecting issue resolution
  • ▸ Inaccurate identification of complex issues during the review process
  • ▸ Delays in archiving and managing reports
  • ▸ Miscommunication with stakeholders regarding the audit report
