Identification

  • ID RM-RV-03
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Waitlist & Tentative Booking Management
  • Trigger Customer requests a reservation that is not immediately available due to room availability constraints.
  • Outcome Successful management of waitlist and tentative bookings, ensuring customer satisfaction and optimizing revenue.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for identifying room availability
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes for creating waitlist entries
  • ▸ Daily review of waitlist requests within 1 hour
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per booking for confirming tentative bookings
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for updating customer on booking confirmation
  • ▸ Weekly review of waitlist policies within 1 hour

Description

Process for managing waitlists and tentative bookings at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate real-time room availability data leading to missed reservations
  • ▸ Delays in notifying customers about their waitlist status affecting reputation
  • ▸ Handling exceptions for urgent waitlist requests without impacting other tasks
  • ▸ Assessing the impact of tentative bookings on overall occupancy without overbooking
  • ▸ Ensuring timely and accurate customer communication for both waitlist updates and confirmations
