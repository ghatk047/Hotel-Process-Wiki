ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID RM-RV-06
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process CRS-PMS Synchronisation
  • Trigger A new reservation is made through SynXis CRS or an existing reservation is updated.
  • Outcome Successful synchronization of reservation data between SynXis CRS and Oracle OPERA Cloud, ensuring accurate room availability, rates, and guest information across all systems.

KPIs

  • ▸ Synchronization time less than 5 min
  • ▸ Error rate in synchronization process less than 1%
  • ▸ Data consistency across systems greater than 98%
  • ▸ Front desk update time less than 2 min

Description

This process ensures seamless synchronization between the CRS (Central Reservation System) and PMS (Property Management System) at a Marriott full-service hotel, optimizing reservations management and revenue generation.

Risks

  • ▸ Network connectivity issues affecting real-time updates
  • ▸ Conflicting data leading to discrepancies in reservation records
  • ▸ Complex reporting requirements causing delays
  • ▸ Unexpected issues during front desk operations
  • ▸ Data consistency problems across multiple systems
