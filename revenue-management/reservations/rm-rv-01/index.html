Identification

  • ID RM-RV-01
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Inbound Reservation Handling
  • Trigger Receiving an inbound reservation request via SynXis CRS or direct phone call
  • Outcome Successful creation and confirmation of the reservation in Oracle OPERA Cloud with all necessary details captured for check-in and stay management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to capture reservation details
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to validate information
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute to confirm availability
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to assign room type and rate

Description

The process RM-RV-01 handles inbound reservations at a Marriott full-service hotel, ensuring accurate capture and management of guest bookings through Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Conflicting room availability during peak seasons
  • ▸ Handling complex requests with multiple amenities
  • ▸ Ensuring real-time synchronization between systems
  • ▸ Managing cancellations or changes during stay
  • ▸ Adjusting forecasts for last-minute changes
