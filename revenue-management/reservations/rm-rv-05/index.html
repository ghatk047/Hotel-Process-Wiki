Identification

  • ID RM-RV-05
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Deposit & Prepayment Processing
  • Trigger Guest makes a reservation with a deposit or prepayment
  • Outcome Successful capture and recording of the deposit or prepayment in the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS, with confirmation sent to the guest.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per reservation
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per transaction
  • ▸ Daily review within 24 hours
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per issue

Description

The process of handling deposit and prepayment processing at a Marriott full-service hotel involves capturing payments from guests during reservation booking and ensuring they are correctly recorded in the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect payment amount captured
  • ▸ Payment confirmation email not sent
  • ▸ Multiple reservations updated simultaneously
  • ▸ Unexpected payment failures
  • ▸ Data inconsistency across systems
