Identification

  • ID RM-RV-02
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Group Reservation & Block Management
  • Trigger Receiving a request for a group reservation from a travel agent, corporate client, or directly from a group leader.
  • Outcome Successful creation and management of the group reservation block with confirmed room assignments, rates, and guest information.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to log the request
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to generate the report
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to confirm details
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to create the block
  • ▸ Daily review of the block status

Description

This process outlines the steps involved in managing group reservations and blocks at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Handling conflicting requests from multiple sources
  • ▸ Ensuring accurate rate calculations
  • ▸ Room availability conflicts
  • ▸ Synchronization issues between systems
  • ▸ Compensating for lost revenue
