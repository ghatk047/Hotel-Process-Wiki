Identification

  • ID RM-RV-08
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Reservations Operations
  • Process Long-Stay & Extended Stay Management
  • Trigger A guest books a long-stay or extended stay reservation through the hotel's reservation system (Oracle OPERA Cloud, SynXis CRS).
  • Outcome Successful management of long-stay and extended stay reservations results in high guest satisfaction, optimized room utilization, and increased revenue.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per reservation review
  • ▸ Daily review within 1 hour of midnight
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per rate adjustment
  • ▸ Within 24 hours of guest departure
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per update
  • ▸ Within 24 hours of guest departure

Description

Managing long-stay and extended stay reservations at a Marriott full-service hotel involves coordinating with guests to ensure their comfort and satisfaction while optimizing revenue.

Risks

  • ▸ Unexpected cancellations or no-shows affecting room utilization
  • ▸ Balancing revenue optimization with guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Maintaining positive guest relations during communication changes
  • ▸ Ensuring timely delivery of amenities and welcome packages
  • ▸ Implementing changes efficiently based on feedback analysis
