Identification

  • ID RM-CH-08
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Channel & Distribution Management
  • Process Rate Parity Monitoring & Enforcement
  • Trigger Daily automated report from Oracle OPERA Cloud highlighting discrepancies in rate parity.
  • Outcome All identified rate discrepancies are resolved, ensuring accurate and consistent pricing across all channels.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 30 min for reviewing daily rate parity report
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour for identifying root causes
  • ▸ Less than 30 min per update for Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • ▸ Less than 15 min for syncing rates with SynXis CRS

Description

The Rate Parity Monitoring & Enforcement process ensures that all hotel rates are consistent across various distribution channels and systems to maximize revenue.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate updates leading to further discrepancies
  • ▸ Communication gaps between revenue managers and channel managers
  • ▸ System synchronization issues causing data inconsistencies
  • ▸ Failure to document findings and actions thoroughly
  • ▸ Policy adjustments not being implemented effectively
