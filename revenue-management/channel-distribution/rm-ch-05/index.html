The process RM-CH-05 outlines the steps for contracting with wholesale and tour operators at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

  • ▸ Delays in initiating the contract request due to incomplete forms
  • ▸ Complex legal negotiations delaying contract approval
  • ▸ Difficulties in reaching agreement on key terms during negotiations
  • ▸ Errors in contract document preparation leading to rework
  • ▸ Technical issues during integration causing data discrepancies
