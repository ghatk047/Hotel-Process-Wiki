ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID RM-CH-02
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Channel & Distribution Management
  • Process GDS Distribution Management
  • Trigger Daily rate changes, new promotions, or updates in hotel inventory
  • Outcome Accurate distribution of room rates and availability across all relevant GDS systems, leading to optimized bookings and increased revenue.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to initiate distribution update
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to verify rate and inventory data
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per GDS for pushing rates
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to generate distribution status report
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per issue to resolve distribution issues
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to update CRM system with accurate data

Description

The GDS Distribution Management process ensures that room rates and availability are accurately distributed across various Global Distribution Systems (GDS) to maximize hotel revenue.

Risks

  • ▸ Manual entry errors leading to incorrect distribution
  • ▸ Network issues affecting data push
  • ▸ System downtime affecting distribution
  • ▸ Complex integration issues
  • ▸ Data synchronization errors
