ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID RM-CH-01
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Channel & Distribution Management
  • Process OTA Channel Management
  • Trigger Daily review of OTA channel performance by the Revenue Management team
  • Outcome Successful completion ensures that OTA channels are up-to-date with accurate room rates, availability, and promotions, leading to increased revenue and guest satisfaction.

KPIs

  • ▸ Completion of OTA performance review within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Accuracy of data synchronization between Oracle OPERA Cloud and OTA systems above 95%
  • ▸ Effective communication with OTA partners measured by response time less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Final approval process completed within 20 minutes

Description

This process outlines the management of OTA channels at a Marriott full-service hotel to ensure optimal room rates and availability are maintained across all online travel agencies.

Risks

  • ▸ Data discrepancies between Oracle OPERA Cloud and OTA systems leading to overbooking risks
  • ▸ Manual rate updates across multiple OTA systems causing inconsistencies
  • ▸ Real-time monitoring of room block reservations failing due to system issues
  • ▸ Effective communication with OTA partners being hindered by technical difficulties
  • ▸ Coordination between different departments for final approval process being delayed
