  • Trigger Daily at 7 AM, triggered by the Revenue Management System (RMS) detecting low occupancy rates or upcoming events that may affect booking patterns.
  • Outcome Optimized metasearch bids and CPC settings across all relevant distribution channels to maximize revenue while maintaining competitive pricing.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to generate occupancy analysis dashboard

  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to review event calendar
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to adjust metasearch bids and CPC settings
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to deploy new bidding strategy

Description

This process outlines how Marriott Bonvoy manages metasearch bidding and CPC (Cost Per Click) management to optimize online hotel bookings through various distribution channels.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate occupancy data leading to incorrect bidding strategies.
  • ▸ Outdated competitor pricing data affecting bidding decisions.
  • ▸ Missing event information leading to missed opportunities.
  • ▸ Manual adjustments causing errors in bidding strategies.
  • ▸ Delayed deployment of new strategies resulting in suboptimal performance.
