  • Trigger Initiation of a new contract with a corporate client or update to existing corporate rate agreements
  • Outcome Successful loading and management of corporate rates, ensuring accurate pricing and availability for contracted clients

KPIs

  • ▸ Rate loading completion within 1 hour
  • ▸ Rate verification accuracy rate above 95%
  • ▸ System integration success rate above 98%
  • ▸ Front desk communication notes updated daily

Description

This process outlines the steps for loading and managing corporate rates at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect contract interpretation leading to rate misloading
  • ▸ System errors causing data loss or corruption during rate updates
  • ▸ Communication breakdown between revenue management and front desk affecting reservations
  • ▸ Market changes not being responded to quickly enough, leading to lost revenue
  • ▸ Client dissatisfaction due to poor communication about rate adjustments
