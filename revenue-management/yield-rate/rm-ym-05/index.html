Identification

  • ID RM-YM-05
  • Domain Revenue Management & Distribution
  • Group Yield & Rate Management
  • Process Group Displacement Analysis
  • Trigger A significant group cancellation or change in reservation dates is detected through Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.
  • Outcome The hotel successfully adjusts its yield management strategy, reallocates resources, and communicates effectively with affected guests to minimize financial impact and maintain guest satisfaction.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for identifying group cancellations
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to notify affected departments
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to adjust yield management strategy
  • ▸ Continuous monitoring of revenue performance

Description

The Group Displacement Analysis process is a structured approach used by Marriott full-service hotels to evaluate and manage the impact of group cancellations or changes on revenue management strategies. This ensures that the hotel can quickly adapt its pricing and availability to maximize occupancy and profitability.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate impact analysis leading to suboptimal decision-making
  • ▸ Poor communication with guests affecting satisfaction and reviews
  • ▸ Lack of real-time monitoring delaying necessary adjustments
  • ▸ Outdated training materials hindering future process improvements
  • ▸ Failure to properly close out the process causing confusion
