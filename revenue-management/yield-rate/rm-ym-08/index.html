  • ID RM-YM-08

  • Process Budget vs Actual Revenue Variance
  • Trigger Monthly end of period (EOY) or quarterly review cycle
  • Outcome A detailed report highlighting variances, root causes, and recommendations for improving revenue management strategies.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data extraction from Oracle OPERA Cloud within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Variance report generation in less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Action plan implementation completed within 2 days
  • ▸ Impact monitoring report prepared weekly within 1 hour

Description

This process involves analyzing and reporting on the variance between budgeted and actual revenue at a Marriott full-service hotel. It helps in identifying discrepancies and taking corrective actions to improve financial performance.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data entry leading to incorrect variance analysis
  • ▸ Resistance from staff during action plan implementation
  • ▸ Delays in final report approval due to conflicting priorities
  • ▸ System downtime affecting data extraction and reporting processes
  • ▸ Insufficient historical data for accurate root cause identification
