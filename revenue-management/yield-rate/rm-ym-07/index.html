Description

This process involves a detailed review of Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR) and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) at a full-service Marriott hotel. It aims to optimize revenue management strategies using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting data extraction and analysis
  • ▸ Manual errors in pricing adjustments leading to revenue loss
  • ▸ Resistance to change in pricing strategies impacting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Low response rates from customers affecting feedback loop effectiveness
  • ▸ Documentation errors causing miscommunication and delays
