Description

Setting the overbooking level at a Marriott full-service hotel involves analyzing demand forecasts, reviewing room availability, and adjusting rates to maximize revenue while ensuring guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate demand forecasts leading to poor overbooking decisions
  • ▸ Outdated room availability data affecting operational accuracy
  • ▸ Incorrect rate updates causing revenue loss
  • ▸ Insufficient communication with front desk leading to confusion
  • ▸ Lack of process documentation resulting in inconsistencies
