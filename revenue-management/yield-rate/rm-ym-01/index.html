  • Trigger Weekly business review meeting or monthly system update
  • Outcome A detailed report with recommended rate adjustments, pickup analysis, and forecasted demand for the upcoming period, leading to improved revenue performance.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data collection within 2 hours
  • ▸ Forecasting accuracy rate above 80%
  • ▸ Rate optimization recommendation implementation within 1 hour
  • ▸ Pickup analysis report generated daily

Description

The Demand Forecasting & Pickup Analysis process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves analyzing historical data and current trends to predict future demand and optimize room rates. This ensures optimal revenue management by adjusting pricing strategies based on anticipated occupancy levels.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data leading to poor forecasting
  • ▸ Synchronization issues between Oracle OPERA Cloud and IDeas G3 RMS
  • ▸ Disagreement among stakeholders on rate adjustments
  • ▸ External market changes not captured in the model
  • ▸ Technical failures affecting system availability
