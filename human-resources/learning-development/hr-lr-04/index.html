  • Trigger Annual performance reviews, new hire onboarding, or identified skill gaps in staff.
  • Outcome Enhanced employee skills, improved operational efficiency, and increased staff confidence across various roles.

KPIs

  • ▸ Training needs assessment completed within less than 2 hours per department
  • ▸ Cross-training and skill development plan developed within less than 1 week
  • ▸ Initial training session conducted within less than 2 hours per session
  • ▸ Training effectiveness report generated within less than 1 week per quarter

Description

A structured process document for cross-training and skill development at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate identification of skill gaps leading to ineffective training programs
  • ▸ Low engagement during training sessions affecting learning outcomes
  • ▸ Difficulty in measuring the impact of training on operational performance
  • ▸ Resistance to change from employees who are reluctant to adopt new skills
  • ▸ Challenges in forecasting future skill development needs based on current trends
