Identification

  • ID HR-LR-06
  • Domain Human Resources & Training
  • Group Learning & Development
  • Process Health & Safety Training
  • Trigger Annual review of health and safety policies or upon hiring new staff
  • Outcome All employees are certified in health and safety training, reducing the risk of accidents and ensuring compliance with regulations.

KPIs

  • ▸ Training sessions completed on time: 95%
  • ▸ Quiz pass rate: 80%
  • ▸ Training records updated within 24 hours of completion: 100%
  • ▸ Effective communication of training outcomes to employees: 90%

Description

Health & Safety Training process at a Marriott full-service hotel ensures all employees are trained on safety protocols and emergency procedures.

Risks

  • ▸ Non-compliance with health and safety regulations due to outdated policies
  • ▸ Low employee attendance during training sessions
  • ▸ Inaccurate recording or analysis of quiz results
  • ▸ Failure to communicate training outcomes effectively to stakeholders
  • ▸ Resistance to change in the training process
