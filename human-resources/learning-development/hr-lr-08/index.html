Description

This process measures the effectiveness of training programs at a Marriott full-service hotel by tracking participant feedback, performance metrics, and engagement levels.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in initiating measurements can lead to missed opportunities for continuous improvement.
  • ▸ Low response rates can skew the effectiveness data.
  • ▸ Delayed feedback can impact the timeliness of analysis.
  • ▸ Inaccurate performance data can lead to incorrect conclusions.
  • ▸ Poor presentation can lead to misinterpretation of results.
