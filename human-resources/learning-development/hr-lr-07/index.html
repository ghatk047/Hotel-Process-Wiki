Description

HR-LR-07 Service Excellence & Upselling Training is a structured process designed to enhance service quality and upselling skills among Marriott Bonvoy hotel staff. This training aims to improve guest satisfaction and drive revenue through effective communication and customer engagement.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate identification of training needs based on data analysis
  • ▸ Engagement levels dropping in virtual training settings
  • ▸ Difficulty in addressing individual questions during live Q&A sessions
  • ▸ Challenges in gathering comprehensive and actionable feedback
  • ▸ Resistance to change when implementing improvements
