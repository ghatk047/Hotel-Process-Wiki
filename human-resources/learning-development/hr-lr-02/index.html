KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per new hire for welcome orientation

  • ▸ Less than 2 hours per new hire for practice session
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per new hire for onboarding completion

Description

This process outlines the training of new hires in brand standards at a full-service Marriott hotel. It ensures that all new employees are familiar with Marriott's policies, procedures, and systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in system access setup due to technical issues
  • ▸ Inadequate feedback collection leading to ineffective training adjustments
  • ▸ Assessment results not accurately reflecting training objectives
