  • Outcome Successful completion results in accurate payroll processing, timely benefit distribution, and updated employee records in all relevant systems.

KPIs

  • ▸ Payroll processing time: less than 4 hours per month
  • ▸ Benefit distribution time: less than 4 hours per month
  • ▸ Employee record update accuracy: 99.5%
  • ▸ Paycheck distribution time: less than 24 hours

Description

The HR-TL-04 process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves managing payroll and benefits administration, ensuring accurate processing and timely distribution of compensation and employee benefits.

Risks

  • ▸ Data entry errors in Oracle OPERA Cloud can lead to incorrect payroll calculations.
  • ▸ Delays in benefit plan updates can cause employee dissatisfaction.
  • ▸ Non-compliance with labor laws due to inaccurate employee records.
