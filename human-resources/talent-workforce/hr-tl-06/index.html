  • Group Talent & Workforce Management
  • Process Employee Relations & Grievance Handling
  • Trigger An employee submits a formal grievance or complaint through the HR department or directly to management.
  • Outcome A documented resolution that addresses the employee's concerns, maintains confidentiality, and adheres to Marriott International's policies and procedures.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for complaint registration
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for initial assessment
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours for investigation completion
  • ▸ Within 7 days for employee response to notification

Description

HR-TL-06 outlines the process for handling employee relations and grievances at a full-service Marriott hotel. This ensures timely resolution of issues, maintaining a positive work environment, and compliance with company policies.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent handling of grievances leading to unfair treatment
  • ▸ Delayed notifications causing further dissatisfaction among employees
  • ▸ Poorly documented investigations affecting the integrity of the process
  • ▸ Lack of clear communication during the resolution process
