Description

HR-TL-02 is a structured onboarding and induction programme designed to ensure new hires at Marriott Bonvoy hotels are fully prepared and integrated into their roles. This process covers three phases: initiation, execution, and closeout.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in sending welcome emails due to system issues
  • ▸ Technical problems with Oracle OPERA Cloud affecting training sessions
  • ▸ Difficulty in adapting to new systems like Oracle MICROS Simphony
  • ▸ Subjective performance reviews without clear metrics
  • ▸ Non-response rates leading to incomplete feedback
