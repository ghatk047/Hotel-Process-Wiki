Identification

  • ID HR-EW-07
  • Domain Human Resources & Training
  • Group Employee Wellbeing & Engagement
  • Process Exit Interview & Turnover Analysis
  • Trigger The initiation of this process is triggered by an employee's resignation or termination notice being recorded in the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS system.
  • Outcome Successful completion of this process produces a comprehensive report detailing the reasons for employee departure, actionable insights, and recommendations to improve employee wellbeing and engagement. This report helps in implementing strategies to reduce turnover and enhance overall hotel performance.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 24 hours from resignation to initiation
  • ▸ Less than 3 business days from initiation to scheduling
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour from scheduling to preparation
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per interview
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours per transcribing
  • ▸ Less than 1 business day from analysis to identification

Description

HR-EW-07 is a structured process document designed to conduct exit interviews and analyze turnover at Marriott full-service hotels. This process aims to gather valuable insights into employee satisfaction, identify areas for improvement, and reduce turnover rates.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in initiating the exit interview process can lead to missed opportunities for gathering valuable feedback.
  • ▸ Difficulty in securing a suitable time for the exit interview due to employee availability and conflicting schedules.
  • ▸ Handling sensitive topics and ensuring a comfortable environment for the departing employee.
  • ▸ Accuracy and completeness of transcriptions to ensure data integrity.
  • ▸ Interpreting qualitative data and identifying key themes and patterns.
