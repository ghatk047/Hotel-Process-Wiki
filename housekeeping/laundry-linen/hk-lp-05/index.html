ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-LP-05
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process Guest Laundry Service
  • Trigger A guest submits a request for laundry service through the front desk or online booking platform.
  • Outcome Clean linens are delivered to the guest's room within the agreed-upon time frame, and the guest is notified of the completion status.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to log the request
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to assign the task
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to collect linens
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to deliver linens

Description

The Guest Laundry Service process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves handling guest requests for laundry services, ensuring timely collection, processing, and delivery of clean linens.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate laundry requests leading to incorrect processing
  • ▸ Delays in linen collection due to room access issues
  • ▸ Quality issues with washed linens affecting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Overloading of laundry staff during peak periods
  • ▸ System downtime impacting the ability to log and track tasks
