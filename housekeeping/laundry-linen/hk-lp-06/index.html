ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-LP-06
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process Linen Quality Control & Discard
  • Trigger Daily inspection of linen by Housekeeping staff or upon receiving returned linens from guests.
  • Outcome Linen items are either cleaned and reused, placed in the laundry for further processing, or discarded if they do not meet quality standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per item inspection time
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per batch cleaning instructions entry
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per batch linen delivery to laundry
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per week for process review and adjustment

Description

A structured process document for Linen Quality Control & Discard at a Marriott full-service hotel. This ensures that linens meet quality standards and are discarded properly to maintain guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent quality standards leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Delays in the laundry process affecting room availability
  • ▸ Improper disposal of discarded linens causing environmental issues
  • ▸ Manual data entry errors leading to inventory discrepancies
  • ▸ Subjective judgment in quality assessment causing inconsistencies
