Description

The Linen Collection & Sorting process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves collecting dirty linens from guest rooms and sorting them based on color and type for laundry.

Risks

  • ▸ Contamination of linens during collection and sorting
  • ▸ Damage to linens during transport
  • ▸ Inaccurate tracking leading to stockouts or overstocking
  • ▸ Delays in laundry pickup affecting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Handling soiled linens safely without causing accidents
