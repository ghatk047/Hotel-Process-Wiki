ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-LP-02
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process Commercial Laundry Processing
  • Trigger Detection of dirty or soiled commercial laundry items by housekeeping staff during room inspections and other cleaning activities.
  • Outcome Cleaned and properly sorted commercial laundry items are delivered back to the originating department, ensuring that all linens and uniforms are available for use.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per room inspection
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per bag of laundry
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per cart load
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per delivery

Description

The Commercial Laundry Processing process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves managing the collection, sorting, and delivery of commercial laundry items. This ensures that all linens and uniforms are cleaned efficiently and returned to their respective departments in a timely manner.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate identification and classification of dirty laundry items
  • ▸ Sorting items incorrectly leading to color bleeding or improper cleaning
  • ▸ Damage to delicate items during transportation or processing
