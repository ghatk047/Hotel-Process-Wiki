Description

The process for handling uniform laundering and issuing new uniforms at a full-service Marriott hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect inventory checks leading to inaccurate shortage identification
  • ▸ Delays in logging requests causing further shortages
  • ▸ Incorrect sorting of uniforms damaging them during the laundry process
  • ▸ Missed defects in inspected uniforms leading to guest complaints
  • ▸ Incorrect issuance of uniforms causing confusion among staff
