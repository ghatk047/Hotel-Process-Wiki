ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-LP-08
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process External Laundry Vendor Management
  • Trigger Initiated by the Executive Housekeeper upon identification of linen needs that exceed internal capacity or quality standards.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in timely, high-quality laundry services with accurate inventory tracking and cost management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Laundry order preparation time: Less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Vendor response time: Within 1 hour
  • ▸ Daily laundry status checks: Daily check-ins
  • ▸ Inventory update accuracy: Within 1 day of delivery
  • ▸ Quality inspection time per batch: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Final inventory review completion: Within 1 day

Description

This process outlines the management of external laundry vendors at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. It ensures efficient linen handling and maintenance to meet guest expectations.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate room status reporting leading to over/underestimation of laundry needs
  • ▸ Vendor communication delays affecting order processing and delivery timelines
  • ▸ Consistency issues in laundry quality impacting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Vendor performance tracking errors leading to suboptimal vendor selection
  • ▸ Final inventory counts inaccuracies resulting in stockouts or excess inventory
