Identification

  • ID HK-LP-07
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process Linen Par Level Management
  • Trigger Weekly review of linen inventory levels by the Executive Housekeeper
  • Outcome Maintaining accurate linen inventory levels to meet guest needs and operational efficiency.

KPIs

  • ▸ Inventory review completion within 15 minutes
  • ▸ Order placement and update within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Supplier delivery monitoring within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Linen inspection and data entry within 20-30 minutes per delivery
  • ▸ Report generation and approval within 10-15 minutes

Description

HK-LP-07 is a process to manage linen par levels in full-service Marriott hotels, ensuring optimal inventory levels and efficient laundry operations.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate inventory data leading to over/understocking
  • ▸ Supplier delays or stockouts affecting operations
  • ▸ Data synchronization issues between systems
  • ▸ Damaged or incorrect items received during delivery
  • ▸ Approval delays impacting operational planning
