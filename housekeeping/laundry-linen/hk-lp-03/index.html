ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-LP-03
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Laundry & Linen Operations
  • Process Linen Inventory Management
  • Trigger Daily inventory check or low stock alert from Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • Outcome Accurate linen inventory levels maintained, timely replenishment of low stocks, and efficient tracking of linen usage and cleaning cycles.

KPIs

  • ▸ Daily inventory check completion time: less than 5 minutes
  • ▸ Order placement confirmation time: less than 15 minutes
  • ▸ Linen inspection and update time: less than 1 hour
  • ▸ Usage analysis report generation time: less than 20 minutes

Description

This process manages the inventory of linens in a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other relevant systems. It ensures that linens are adequately stocked, tracked, and managed to maintain guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Supplier delays in order processing
  • ▸ Lost or delayed orders
  • ▸ Manual data entry errors leading to inaccurate inventory records
  • ▸ Inaccurate reporting due to system issues
  • ▸ Over or underestimation of linen usage
