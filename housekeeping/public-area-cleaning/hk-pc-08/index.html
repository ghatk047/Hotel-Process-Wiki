Description

The Deep Clean Scheduling & Execution process ensures that all public areas and high-traffic rooms in a Marriott full-service hotel are thoroughly cleaned on a regular basis to maintain hygiene standards and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate room status leading to inefficient cleaning schedules
  • ▸ Insufficient supplies causing delays in cleaning tasks
  • ▸ Poorly scheduled tasks resulting in overlapping duties and inefficiencies
  • ▸ Missed inspections leading to unclean rooms and guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Outdated room statuses causing scheduling conflicts
