  • Process Pool & Fitness Area Cleaning
  • Trigger Daily check-in by the Executive Housekeeper or upon request from the Spa Manager
  • Outcome Clean and sanitized pool and fitness areas ready for guest use, with all equipment functioning properly.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to update the cleaning schedule
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to clean the pool area
  • ▸ Less than 45 minutes to clean the fitness area
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to inspect and maintain equipment
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to complete the daily cleaning status report
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to communicate with the Spa Manager

Description

The process ensures that the pool and fitness area at a Marriott full-service hotel are thoroughly cleaned and maintained to meet health and safety standards.

Risks

  • ▸ Stockouts of essential cleaning products leading to incomplete cleanings
  • ▸ Neglecting routine maintenance causing equipment breakdowns
  • ▸ Inflexible scheduling resulting in overburdened staff
  • ▸ Lack of communication between departments affecting service quality
  • ▸ Manual logging errors impacting data accuracy
