Identification

  • ID HK-PC-02
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Public Area & Cleaning Operations
  • Process Corridor & Elevator Cleaning
  • Trigger Daily at the start of housekeeping operations or upon request from guest services or property management.
  • Outcome Clean and well-maintained corridors and elevators that meet Marriott's high standards for cleanliness and safety.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for initiating the cleaning schedule and reporting status.
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes for assigning tasks and updating logs.
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for reviewing and adjusting the cleaning schedule.
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for preparing supplies and documenting.

Description

The Corridor & Elevator Cleaning process ensures that public areas of a Marriott full-service hotel are clean, safe, and presentable to guests. This includes daily cleaning of corridors and elevators, maintenance checks, and reporting.

Risks

  • ▸ Inadequate supply levels leading to incomplete cleaning duties.
  • ▸ Overlooking critical issues during inspections, resulting in guest complaints.
  • ▸ Incorrect reporting or documentation, causing unresolved problems.
  • ▸ Failure to adapt the cleaning schedule based on daily findings.
