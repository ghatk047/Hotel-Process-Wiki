Description

This process outlines the steps involved in cleaning the lobby and entrance area of a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. The goal is to ensure that the public areas are clean, presentable, and safe for guests.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate scheduling leading to missed tasks
  • ▸ Running out of essential supplies
  • ▸ Difficulty in accessing hard-to-reach areas
  • ▸ Floors becoming slippery due to improper drying
  • ▸ Delays in reporting and resolving maintenance issues
