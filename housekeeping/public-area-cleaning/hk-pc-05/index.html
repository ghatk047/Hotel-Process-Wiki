  • Trigger Scheduled daily by the Housekeeping Department, or triggered by guest complaints or inspections.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 min per task scheduling

  • ▸ Less than 10 min per restroom cleaning
  • ▸ Less than 2 min per inspection report
  • ▸ Less than 5 min per audit findings documentation

Description

HK-PC-05 Public Restroom Cleaning & Audit process ensures that public restrooms in a Marriott full-service hotel are cleaned and inspected regularly to maintain high standards of hygiene and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Insufficient staffing for scheduled tasks
  • ▸ Supply shortages or equipment malfunctions
  • ▸ Resource allocation for corrective actions
  • ▸ Delays in maintenance response
  • ▸ Incomplete or incorrect documentation
