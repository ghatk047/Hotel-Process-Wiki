Identification

  • ID HK-PC-04
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Public Area & Cleaning Operations
  • Process Restaurant & Banquet Hall Cleaning
  • Trigger Daily end-of-shift report from housekeeping staff indicating completed tasks, or scheduled maintenance checklists.
  • Outcome A clean, hygienic restaurant and banquet hall ready for the next day's operations, with all cleaning activities documented and reported.

KPIs

  • ▸ Cleaning schedule initiated within 5 minutes of end-of-shift report
  • ▸ Tasks assigned to cleaners within 3 minutes of cleaning schedule creation
  • ▸ Tables and chairs cleaned in less than 10 minutes per set
  • ▸ Floors cleaned in less than 15 minutes per area
  • ▸ Restrooms cleaned in less than 10 minutes each

Description

This process outlines the cleaning procedures for the restaurant and banquet hall at a full-service Marriott hotel. It ensures that all areas are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized to maintain high standards of hygiene and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate reporting from housekeeping staff leading to missed cleaning tasks
  • ▸ Overloading cleaners with tasks, causing rushed and incomplete work
  • ▸ Insufficient stock of cleaning supplies
  • ▸ Difficult-to-reach areas or heavy furniture during cleaning
  • ▸ Delayed reporting leading to operational disruptions
