Identification

  • ID HK-GC-04
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Guest Room Cleaning & Turndown
  • Process Room Inspection & Quality Check
  • Trigger Scheduled daily or after guest departure
  • Outcome Clean, well-maintained rooms ready for the next guest, documented inspection results, and resolved issues

KPIs

  • ▸ Room inspection time: less than 30 min
  • ▸ Corrective action completion rate: 95% within 24 hours
  • ▸ Data entry accuracy: 98%
  • ▸ Notification delay to Front Desk: less than 10 min

Description

The HK-GC-04 process involves a detailed room inspection and quality check to ensure guest satisfaction and hotel standards are met. This process is initiated by housekeeping staff and executed using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS, with outcomes including documented issues and corrective actions.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent inspection standards across housekeeping staff
  • ▸ Delayed corrective actions leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Data entry errors in Oracle OPERA Cloud affecting room availability
  • ▸ Supervisors overlooking critical issues during inspections
  • ▸ Housekeepers neglecting final checks, resulting in undetected problems
