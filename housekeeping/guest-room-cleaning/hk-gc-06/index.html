  • Group Guest Room Cleaning & Turndown
  • Process Minibar Restocking & Billing
  • Trigger Guest check-in or upon request from housekeeping staff
  • Outcome Minibar is fully stocked with approved beverages and snacks, and accurate charges are reflected in the guest's account.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per room for stock level analysis and verification
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for item ordering
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per room for minibar stocking
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per room for billing confirmation and process completion

Description

The process of restocking minibars and billing guests for items consumed at Marriott full-service hotels.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect or incomplete inventory data leading to over or under stocking
  • ▸ Delays in item delivery affecting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Errors in billing leading to disputes with guests
  • ▸ Outdated inventory records causing stock discrepancies
  • ▸ Incomplete process documentation resulting in operational inefficiencies
