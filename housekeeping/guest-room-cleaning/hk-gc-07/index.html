ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID HK-GC-07
  • Domain Housekeeping & Rooms Management
  • Group Guest Room Cleaning & Turndown
  • Process Lost & Found Management
  • Trigger A guest reports a lost item to housekeeping or front desk staff.
  • Outcome The lost item is located, documented, and either returned to the guest or stored securely for retrieval.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to log the report
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to notify
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to assign
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per room searched
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to update
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per retrieval

Description

This process outlines how a Marriott full-service hotel manages lost and found items. It ensures that lost items are tracked, reported, and returned to guests efficiently.

Risks

  • ▸ Multiple reports overwhelming staff
  • ▸ Inefficient search coordination
  • ▸ Handling sensitive information improperly
  • ▸ Coordinating with guest schedules difficulties
  • ▸ Maintaining accurate logs
