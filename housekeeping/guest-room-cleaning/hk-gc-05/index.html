  • Trigger Daily check-in of guests, end-of-day housekeeping report, or inventory alerts from Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in all guest rooms having an adequate supply of clean linens and towels, with minimal waste and efficient use of resources.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per room type for inventory review
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per day for ordering linens
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per order for receiving linens
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes per batch for sorting and inspecting linens
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per month for process improvement planning

Description

The Linen & Towel Management process at a Marriott full-service hotel ensures that guest rooms are provided with clean and sufficient linens and towels for optimal guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate inventory counts leading to stockouts or overstocking.
  • ▸ Delays in supplier delivery affecting room readiness.
  • ▸ Damaged or incorrect items received from suppliers.
