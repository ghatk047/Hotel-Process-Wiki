Identification

  • ID FM-RM-05
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Repair Completion & Room Return to Service
  • Trigger Completion of a reactive maintenance work order
  • Outcome The room is returned to service with all repairs completed and documented.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for verifying repair completion
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes for updating room status in PMS
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for notifying departments
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for final room inspection
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for updating revenue management systems

Description

This process outlines the steps involved in completing a repair and returning a room to service at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent documentation of repair steps leading to incomplete repairs
  • ▸ Room status not updating automatically causing delays in service
  • ▸ Delayed communication between departments affecting room availability
  • ▸ Inaccurate room availability updates impacting reservations
  • ▸ Synchronization issues between CRS and RMS causing booking conflicts
