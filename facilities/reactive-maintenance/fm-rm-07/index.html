ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-RM-07
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Capital Works & Renovation Project Management
  • Trigger Initiation of a capital works or renovation project by Facilities Management (FM) or Engineering Department based on identified needs or scheduled maintenance.
  • Outcome Successful completion of the capital works or renovation project, with all work orders executed as planned, and documentation updated in relevant systems.

KPIs

  • ▸ Project initiation within 2 hours
  • ▸ Vendor selection within 5 days
  • ▸ Work order creation less than 3 days
  • ▸ Work order execution less than 5 days per work order
  • ▸ Final inspection within 1 day
  • ▸ Documentation update less than 2 days

Description

The process document outlines the management of capital works and renovation projects at a full-service Marriott hotel. It ensures that all necessary steps are followed to complete projects efficiently while maintaining high standards of guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in vendor selection due to complex requirements
  • ▸ Inaccurate work order creation leading to project delays
  • ▸ Non-compliance with Marriott's quality standards during final inspection
  • ▸ Insufficient budget allocation affecting project execution
  • ▸ Poor communication between departments causing misalignment
