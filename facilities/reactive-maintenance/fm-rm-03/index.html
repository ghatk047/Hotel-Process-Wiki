  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Work Order Prioritisation & Assignment
  • Trigger Detection of a facility issue via guest reports, staff observations, or automated systems
  • Outcome Work order is prioritized based on severity and urgency, and assigned to the appropriate technician for timely resolution.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to log the issue
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to assess the issue
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to queue the work order
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes to complete the work order
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to review the work order

Description

This process outlines how reactive maintenance work orders are prioritized and assigned at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting reporting and tracking
  • ▸ Inconsistent prioritization criteria leading to delayed resolutions
  • ▸ Skill mismatches between issues and technicians causing inefficiencies
  • ▸ Technician not receiving notifications resulting in missed deadlines
  • ▸ Poor quality of work leading to re-assignments and increased costs
