Identification

  • ID FM-RM-06
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Maintenance Cost Tracking & Reporting
  • Trigger Initiation of a reactive maintenance work order in Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.
  • Outcome Successful completion produces accurate cost tracking, detailed reporting, and adherence to budget constraints.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per maintenance report
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per work order assignment
  • ▸ Less than 60 minutes per maintenance task
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per invoice creation
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per cost review
  • ▸ Less than 20 minutes per maintenance report generation

Description

This process tracks and reports maintenance costs at a Marriott full-service hotel to ensure financial transparency and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Delayed reporting due to unclear issue descriptions
  • ▸ Inefficient allocation of technicians
  • ▸ Inadequate inventory of spare parts
  • ▸ Manual data entry errors leading to incorrect cost calculations
  • ▸ Overlooking discrepancies in costs during the review process
