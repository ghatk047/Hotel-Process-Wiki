ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-RM-08
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Asset Register & Lifecycle Management
  • Trigger Initiated by a Facilities Manager upon identification of a new asset purchase or existing asset requiring maintenance.
  • Outcome A comprehensive asset register is maintained with updated information on all assets, ensuring efficient reactive maintenance and work order management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per asset for Asset Identification and Registration
  • ▸ Less than 4 minutes per request for Maintenance Request Creation
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per task for Maintenance Execution
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per asset for Decommissioning and Retirement

Description

The process ensures accurate tracking and management of hotel assets throughout their lifecycle, from procurement to decommissioning.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting asset tracking and maintenance processes.
  • ▸ Inconsistent maintenance schedules leading to guest dissatisfaction.
  • ▸ Data integrity issues during the decommissioning process.
