Description

This process outlines the emergency repair response procedure at a Marriott full-service hotel, ensuring quick and efficient resolution of facility issues that impact guest comfort and safety.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate initial assessment leading to ineffective repairs
  • ▸ Delays in part delivery due to procurement issues
  • ▸ Suboptimal repair techniques causing recurring problems
  • ▸ Outdated PMS information resulting in confusion
  • ▸ Lack of communication with guests leading to dissatisfaction
