Identification

  • ID FM-RM-04
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Reactive Maintenance & Work Orders
  • Process Contractor & Vendor Call-Out Management
  • Trigger Reactive maintenance request from Facilities & Engineering team
  • Outcome Successful completion of the reactive maintenance task by the contracted vendor, with all records updated in the relevant systems.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to create work order
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to assign vendor
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to schedule call-out
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to send notification
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to complete task

Description

This process outlines how to manage contractor and vendor call-outs at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other relevant systems.

Risks

  • ▸ System errors during work order creation and updates
  • ▸ Vendor availability conflicts or delays
  • ▸ Failed notifications due to vendor contact issues
  • ▸ Payment delays or errors in invoicing
  • ▸ Log entry errors or omissions
