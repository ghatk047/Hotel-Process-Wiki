Identification

  • ID FM-EM-02
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process GRMS & BMS Optimisation
  • Trigger Annual review of energy consumption data and identification of inefficiencies in GRMS and BMS operations.
  • Outcome Improved energy efficiency, reduced operational costs, enhanced guest satisfaction through optimized room temperature control, and compliance with environmental standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Completion of audit within 2 weeks
  • ▸ Implementation of GRMS/BMS adjustments within 5 days
  • ▸ Daily review of monitoring report within 1 hour
  • ▸ Final audit review and optimization report within 1 week
  • ▸ Stakeholder presentation within 1 day
  • ▸ Change documentation completed within 1 week

Description

Optimizing Guest Room Management System (GRMS) and Building Management System (BMS) to enhance energy efficiency and guest comfort at a Marriott full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data collection during the initial audit
  • ▸ Disruption of guest comfort during system adjustments
  • ▸ Complex communication of technical information to stakeholders
  • ▸ Resistance to change among staff
  • ▸ Failure to meet optimization goals due to unforeseen issues
