  • Process Green Certification Management
  • Trigger Annual review of energy usage data or when new green initiatives are introduced
  • Outcome Successful completion produces a comprehensive report on energy efficiency improvements, compliance with green certification standards, and recommendations for further actions.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 days to complete the initial review

Description

The Green Certification Management process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves monitoring and managing energy consumption to achieve sustainability goals.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent data reporting across different departments
  • ▸ Lack of alignment between sustainability goals and operational needs
  • ▸ Resistance to change among staff
  • ▸ Limited budget for upgrades
  • ▸ Delays in external certification processes
