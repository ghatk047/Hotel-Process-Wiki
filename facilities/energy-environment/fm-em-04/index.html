Identification

  • ID FM-EM-04
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process Waste Management & Recycling
  • Trigger Weekly review of waste generation and recycling rates by the Executive Housekeeper
  • Outcome A successful completion ensures that all waste is properly categorized, recycled, or disposed of in an environmentally friendly manner, meeting local regulations and reducing the hotel's ecological footprint.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to review the waste generation report
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour for training sessions on waste categories
  • ▸ Daily monitoring with less than 15 minutes per check of waste segregation compliance
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour to collect and sort waste

Description

This process outlines the waste management and recycling procedures at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring compliance with environmental standards and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent data reporting in Oracle OPERA Cloud leading to inaccurate waste management decisions
  • ▸ Low staff engagement in recycling programs resulting in improper waste segregation
  • ▸ High volume of waste leading to non-compliance with waste segregation protocols
  • ▸ Inadequate storage space for sorted waste causing overflow and contamination issues
