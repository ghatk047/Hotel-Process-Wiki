ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-EM-08
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process Environmental Incident Response
  • Trigger Detection of an environmental incident such as water leak, gas leak, or other environmental hazards.
  • Outcome Successful containment, mitigation, and resolution of the environmental incident with minimal disruption to hotel operations and guest safety.

KPIs

  • ▸ Incident detection within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Containment measures implemented within 15 minutes
  • ▸ Communication plan developed within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Cleanup and restoration completed within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Post-incident review conducted within 1 hour
  • ▸ Training and awareness plan updated within 1 week

Description

This process outlines the steps to be taken in response to an environmental incident at a Marriott full-service hotel. It ensures that the incident is handled efficiently and effectively, minimizing impact on guests and the environment.

Risks

  • ▸ Delayed detection of the incident leading to escalation
  • ▸ Inaccurate assessment resulting in improper response
  • ▸ Failure to contain the incident causing further damage
  • ▸ Miscommunication with guests leading to dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Non-compliance with local regulations regarding notification of authorities
