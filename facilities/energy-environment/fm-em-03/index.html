ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-EM-03
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process Water Conservation Management
  • Trigger Monthly review of water usage data from Oracle OPERA Cloud, identification of anomalies in consumption patterns, or receipt of external environmental compliance notifications.
  • Outcome Implementation of effective water-saving measures, reduction in water usage by at least 5%, and ensuring all hotel operations comply with local water conservation regulations.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5% reduction in water usage per month
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes for reviewing water usage data
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours for developing a water conservation plan
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour for communicating the plan to staff

Description

A structured process document for managing water conservation at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent data reporting from Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • ▸ Resistance to change among staff
  • ▸ Compatibility issues with existing hotel systems
  • ▸ Resource constraints for optimization
  • ▸ Complexity in reporting tools
