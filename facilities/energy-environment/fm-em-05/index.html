ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-EM-05
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process Carbon Footprint Tracking & Reporting
  • Trigger Annual environmental audit or quarterly energy review
  • Outcome A comprehensive report detailing the hotel's carbon emissions, reduction strategies, and compliance with sustainability goals.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data collection accuracy rate > 95%
  • ▸ Reduction strategy implementation within 3 months
  • ▸ Final report preparation time < 1 week
  • ▸ Stakeholder approval time < 2 days

Description

This process outlines the steps for tracking and reporting a hotel's carbon footprint at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other relevant systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent data quality across systems
  • ▸ Technical failures during reduction actions
  • ▸ Resource allocation challenges for sustainability initiatives
  • ▸ Delays in stakeholder approvals
  • ▸ Data interpretation errors leading to incorrect strategies
