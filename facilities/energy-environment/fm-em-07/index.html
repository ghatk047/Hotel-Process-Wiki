ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-EM-07
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Energy & Environment Management
  • Process Sustainability Supplier Assessment
  • Trigger Initiated by the Sustainability Manager upon identification of new or existing suppliers that need evaluation.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in a comprehensive sustainability assessment report for each supplier, leading to informed procurement decisions and improved environmental performance.

KPIs

  • ▸ Assessment initiation within 1 hour (90%)
  • ▸ Supplier response rate above 80%
  • ▸ On-site audit completion within 2 weeks (95%)
  • ▸ Final report compilation within 1 day (90%)
  • ▸ Procurement strategy update within 1 week (95%)

Description

A process to assess sustainability suppliers at a Marriott full-service hotel, ensuring compliance with environmental standards and reducing operational impact.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in initiating the assessment due to system integration issues.
  • ▸ Inconsistent team availability for assessments.
  • ▸ Low response rates from suppliers.
  • ▸ Incomplete or inaccurate supplier data.
  • ▸ Logistical challenges for on-site visits.
