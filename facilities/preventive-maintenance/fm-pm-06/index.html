ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-PM-06
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Preventive Maintenance
  • Process Elevator & Escalator Maintenance
  • Trigger Scheduled preventive maintenance checklists are triggered by the Facilities & Engineering team based on manufacturer recommendations and historical data.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in documented compliance with maintenance schedules, operational readiness of elevators and escalators, and reduced risk of breakdowns or accidents.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 min for reviewing and updating maintenance logs
  • ▸ Less than 30 min for inspecting elevators/escalators
  • ▸ Less than 60 min for performing maintenance tasks
  • ▸ Less than 15 min for testing equipment

Description

This process outlines the maintenance activities for elevators and escalators at a full-service Marriott hotel to ensure safe and efficient operation.

Risks

  • ▸ Neglecting safety protocols during inspection
  • ▸ Inadequate training leading to improper maintenance procedures
  • ▸ Failure to thoroughly test equipment before reactivation
  • ▸ Delayed updates to the maintenance log affecting future planning
  • ▸ Lack of transparency in reporting maintenance activities to management
