ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-PM-01
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Preventive Maintenance
  • Process Planned Maintenance Scheduling
  • Trigger Scheduled weekly by the Facilities Manager based on the previous week's maintenance logs and upcoming seasonal needs.
  • Outcome A comprehensive schedule of preventive maintenance tasks is created, assigned to specific teams, and integrated into the hotel's daily operations without disrupting guest services.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 60 minutes for task prioritization and assignment
  • ▸ Daily check of task progress in less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Less than 45 minutes per schedule adjustment
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes to communicate the schedule to teams

Description

This process outlines the planned maintenance scheduling at a Marriott full-service hotel, ensuring that all facilities and engineering tasks are systematically managed to maintain high operational standards.

Risks

  • ▸ Technical issues with Oracle OPERA Cloud affecting scheduling
  • ▸ Poor communication leading to missed deadlines
  • ▸ Inability to quickly adapt to changing priorities
  • ▸ Documentation errors leading to confusion
  • ▸ Overlooking critical seasonal maintenance items
