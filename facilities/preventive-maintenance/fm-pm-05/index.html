ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FM-PM-05
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Preventive Maintenance
  • Process Fire Safety Systems Testing
  • Trigger Scheduled maintenance checklist or upon detection of an issue with any fire safety system
  • Outcome All fire safety systems are confirmed to be operational and compliant with local regulations, ensuring guest safety and regulatory compliance.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 30 min for initiating the testing plan
  • ▸ Less than 5 min for notifying the engineering team
  • ▸ Less than 15 min per area for fire alarm tests
  • ▸ Less than 20 min per area for sprinkler system tests

Description

Fire Safety Systems Testing is a critical process to ensure that all fire safety systems are functioning correctly at a Marriott full-service hotel. This includes testing of fire alarms, sprinklers, and emergency lighting.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate or outdated maintenance records leading to missed issues
  • ▸ False alarms or non-functional fire safety systems during testing
  • ▸ Water leakage or non-functional sprinklers causing damage
  • ▸ Flickering or non-functional emergency lights affecting guest safety
  • ▸ Delayed communication between departments impacting the testing process
