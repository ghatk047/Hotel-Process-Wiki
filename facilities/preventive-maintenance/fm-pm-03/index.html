Identification

  • ID FM-PM-03
  • Domain Facilities & Engineering
  • Group Preventive Maintenance
  • Process HVAC & MEP Systems Maintenance
  • Trigger Scheduled preventative maintenance checklist or system failure alert
  • Outcome All HVAC and MEP systems are inspected, maintained, and tested to meet performance standards, ensuring guest satisfaction and energy efficiency.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per work order creation
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes per work order assignment
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per system inspection
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per system maintenance
  • ▸ Less than 15 minutes per system test
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per closure

Description

This process outlines the maintenance of HVAC and MEP systems in a full-service Marriott hotel to ensure guest comfort and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in creating or assigning work orders can lead to missed maintenance windows.
  • ▸ Incorrect assignments can lead to inefficiencies and missed deadlines.
  • ▸ Unexpected issues during maintenance can extend the time required for repairs.
  • ▸ System malfunctions during testing can delay completion of the process.
  • ▸ Missed deadlines for closing work orders can impact future planning.
