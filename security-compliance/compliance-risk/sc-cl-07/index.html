  • Trigger Detection of any crisis event that could impact operations, safety, or compliance.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for identifying crisis events

Description

The SC-CL-07 process ensures a Marriott full-service hotel can effectively manage crises and maintain business continuity by following predefined protocols and leveraging key systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Delayed identification of crisis events leading to prolonged impact
  • ▸ Miscommunication during notification processes resulting in inadequate response
  • ▸ Failure to activate the crisis management plan causing chaos
  • ▸ Inaccurate or delayed communication with guests leading to dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Overlooking critical operational impacts leading to financial losses
