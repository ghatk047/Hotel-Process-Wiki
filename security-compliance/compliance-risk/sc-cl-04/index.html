Description

A structured process document for SC-CL-04 — Brand Standard Compliance Audit at a Marriott full-service hotel. This audit ensures adherence to safety, security, and compliance standards using various Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inadequate data collection from Marriott systems leading to incomplete audits
  • ▸ Non-compliance issues not being accurately classified or prioritized
  • ▸ Lack of coordination across departments during corrective actions implementation
  • ▸ Delayed approval of the final audit report by the General Manager
  • ▸ Failure to address all stakeholders' concerns in the approved audit report
