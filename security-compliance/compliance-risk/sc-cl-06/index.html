ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID SC-CL-06
  • Domain Safety, Security & Compliance
  • Group Compliance & Risk Management
  • Process Insurance & Risk Assessment
  • Trigger Annual review of insurance policies and quarterly risk assessment updates.
  • Outcome A comprehensive insurance policy review and updated risk assessment report, ensuring all risks are identified and mitigated according to Marriott's guidelines.

KPIs

  • ▸ Insurance review checklist completion within 2 days
  • ▸ Risk assessment interviews conducted in less than 30 minutes per interview
  • ▸ Mitigation plan implementation completed within 2 weeks
  • ▸ Final report compiled within 1 day

Description

This process ensures that insurance policies and risk assessments are up-to-date and compliant with Marriott International's safety, security, and compliance standards at a full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Outdated or missing insurance policy documents leading to non-compliance
  • ▸ Inaccurate risk identification during assessments affecting mitigation strategies
  • ▸ Delayed coordination with external insurers for policy amendments
