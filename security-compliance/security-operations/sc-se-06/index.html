Identification

  • ID SC-SE-06

  • Group Security Operations
  • Process Lost Property Management
  • Trigger A guest reports a lost item to the front desk.
  • Outcome The lost property is either returned to the guest or documented as unclaimed with appropriate follow-up actions taken.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to log the report
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to verify details
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to notify guest
  • ▸ Less than 24 hours to dispose of unclaimed property

Description

The process for managing lost property at a Marriott full-service hotel involves identifying the item, documenting it, notifying the guest, and tracking its status until resolution.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect documentation of lost property items leading to miscommunication
  • ▸ Delayed search efforts resulting in prolonged guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Non-compliance with disposal regulations for unclaimed items
