KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 seconds for fire detection activation

Description

This process outlines the fire emergency response and evacuation procedures at a full-service Marriott hotel. It ensures quick and effective action to protect guests and staff while minimizing property damage.

Risks

  • ▸ False alarms leading to unnecessary evacuations
  • ▸ Delays in initiating the evacuation plan causing panic
  • ▸ Lost communication with emergency services delaying response
  • ▸ Unauthorized re-entry by guests or staff causing further damage
  • ▸ Inaccurate damage assessment leading to improper insurance claims
