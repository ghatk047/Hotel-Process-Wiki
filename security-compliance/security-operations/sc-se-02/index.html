KPIs

  • ▸ Alert Generation: Less than 30 seconds
  • ▸ Initial Notification: Less than 1 minute
  • ▸ Incident Assessment: Less than 5 minutes
  • ▸ Response Initiation: Less than 10 minutes
  • ▸ Dispatch Security Personnel: Less than 1 minute
  • ▸ Security Personnel Arrival: Less than 5 minutes

Description

This process outlines the steps for CCTV monitoring and incident detection at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. The goal is to ensure real-time monitoring and swift response to any security incidents.

Risks

  • ▸ False positives leading to unnecessary alerts
  • ▸ Delayed notifications causing response delays
  • ▸ Inaccurate assessment leading to improper response
  • ▸ Lack of clear response protocols
  • ▸ Slow dispatch times or inefficient routing
