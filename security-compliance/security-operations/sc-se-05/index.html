  • Trigger Detection of a suspicious person or package by any hotel staff member.
  • Outcome The hotel ensures the safety and security of guests, employees, and property while following all relevant compliance requirements.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for reporting suspicious activity
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute for notifying the security team
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for assessing the situation
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for initiating evacuation if necessary

Description

This process outlines the steps to be taken by Marriott full-service hotels when a suspicious person or package is detected.

Risks

  • ▸ Delayed reporting due to unfamiliarity with the system
  • ▸ Miscommunication or missed notifications
  • ▸ Inconsistent risk assessment criteria
  • ▸ Coordination issues during evacuation
  • ▸ Resistance to policy changes
