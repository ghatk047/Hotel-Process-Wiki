ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID SC-SE-01
  • Domain Safety, Security & Compliance
  • Group Security Operations
  • Process Access Control & Key Management
  • Trigger Initiation of a new stay for guests or changes in employee roles requiring access adjustments.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in secure and efficient management of guest and staff access keys, ensuring compliance with Marriott's safety, security, and compliance standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for check-in request acknowledgment
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for access key generation
  • ▸ Daily review of access logs within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour for reviewing and adjusting access rights

Description

This process ensures secure access control and key management at a Marriott full-service hotel, leveraging Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other integrated systems to manage guest and staff access efficiently.

Risks

  • ▸ Unauthorized access attempts detected through monitoring systems
  • ▸ Delays in key card production affecting guest check-ins
  • ▸ Inaccurate assignment of access keys leading to security breaches
  • ▸ Lost or damaged keys requiring immediate replacement
  • ▸ Failure to maintain thorough and accurate records for compliance
