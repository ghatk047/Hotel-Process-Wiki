Identification

  • ID FB-BQ-08
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Banqueting & Events Catering
  • Process Group Dining & Special Menus
  • Trigger Receiving a request for a group dining event or special menu from a guest or through the reservation system.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in the successful planning, execution, and billing of the group dining event or special menu according to the client's specifications.

KPIs

  • ▸ Event confirmation within 10 minutes of request (95% of the time)
  • ▸ Special menu items procurement completed within 2 hours (98% of the time)
  • ▸ Event space preparation completed within 4 hours (99% of the time)
  • ▸ Special menu served to guests within 2 hours (97% of the time)
  • ▸ Event bill generated and sent within 1 hour of event completion (95% of the time)

Description

This process outlines the steps for managing group dining and special menus at a Marriott full-service hotel. It involves coordination between various departments to ensure smooth execution of banquets and events catering.

Risks

  • ▸ Overbooking of events leading to space or resource constraints
  • ▸ Supply chain disruptions affecting special menu item availability
  • ▸ Equipment malfunctions during event preparation
  • ▸ Incorrect billing causing client dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Low response rates to follow-up surveys impacting guest feedback
