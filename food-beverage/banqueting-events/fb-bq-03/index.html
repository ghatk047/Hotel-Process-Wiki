Description

Event Day Coordination & Execution process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves coordinating and executing banqueting and events catering services using various Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Technical failures in audio/visual equipment
  • ▸ Ingredient shortages leading to menu changes
  • ▸ Difficult guest management during the event
  • ▸ Incorrect inventory adjustments affecting future events
