Identification

  • ID FB-BQ-06

  • Process Post-Event Breakdown & Reporting
  • Trigger Event conclusion
  • Outcome A comprehensive report detailing the event's financials, attendee feedback, and operational performance is generated for review by management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Budget variance report generated within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Attendee satisfaction report completed in less than 45 minutes
  • ▸ Final event report compiled and distributed within 2 hours
  • ▸ Event case closed in CRM within 30 minutes

Description

This process outlines the steps involved in post-event breakdown and reporting at a Marriott full-service hotel. It ensures that all event-related activities are properly concluded and documented.

Risks

  • ▸ Data entry errors leading to inaccurate financial reports
  • ▸ Integration issues between different Marriott systems affecting data flow
  • ▸ Low response rates to attendee feedback surveys
  • ▸ CRM system downtime impacting event record updates
  • ▸ Manual entry errors during room reservation and attendance tracking
