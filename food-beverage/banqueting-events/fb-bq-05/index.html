Identification

  • ID FB-BQ-05
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations

  • Process Audio-Visual Setup & Support
  • Trigger Event reservation confirmation in Oracle OPERA Cloud
  • Outcome Successful setup and operation of audio-visual equipment during the event, ensuring guest satisfaction and efficient service.

KPIs

  • ▸ Event review time: Less than 5 minutes per event
  • ▸ Resource allocation time: Less than 10 minutes per event
  • ▸ AV setup time: Less than 30 minutes per event
  • ▸ Technical issue response time: Less than 5 minutes per issue
  • ▸ Post-event review time: Less than 1 hour per event
  • ▸ Inventory update time: Less than 10 minutes per event

Description

This process outlines the setup and support of audio-visual equipment for banquets and events at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent event details leading to miscommunication
  • ▸ Overbooking of AV equipment
  • ▸ Technical issues with AV equipment
  • ▸ Poor communication between AV team and event staff
  • ▸ Slow response to technical issues during the event
