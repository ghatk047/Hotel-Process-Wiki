ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FB-BQ-04
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Banqueting & Events Catering
  • Process Catering Menu Planning & Costing
  • Trigger Receiving a new event booking request with specific dietary requirements or special requests from the client
  • Outcome A detailed catering menu plan with associated costs, presented to the client for approval, ensuring alignment with their preferences and financial constraints.

KPIs

  • ▸ Event review time: Less than 5 minutes per event
  • ▸ Menu selection time: Less than 20 minutes per menu
  • ▸ Costing calculation time: Less than 45 minutes per costing
  • ▸ Presentation preparation time: Less than 15 minutes per presentation
  • ▸ Data entry accuracy rate: Greater than 98%
  • ▸ Client approval rate: Greater than 95%

Description

The FB-BQ-04 process involves planning and costing catering menus for banquets and events at a Marriott full-service hotel. This ensures that all food and beverage offerings meet guest expectations while staying within budget.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate dietary requirements leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Menu items not available, causing delays
  • ▸ Costing errors resulting in budget overruns
  • ▸ Difficulty negotiating with clients on menu and costs
  • ▸ Data entry errors affecting event planning
