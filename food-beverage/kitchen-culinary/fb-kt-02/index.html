  • Process Food Purchasing & Receiving
  • Trigger Weekly menu planning by the Executive Chef
  • Outcome Accurate procurement of food items, timely delivery, and updated inventory records in Oracle OPERA Cloud.

KPIs

  • ▸ Forecasting accuracy within 5% of actual usage
  • ▸ Order placement accuracy rate above 98%
  • ▸ Inventory verification completion time less than 1 hour
  • ▸ Vendor performance review cycle time under 2 hours

Description

The Food Purchasing & Receiving process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves ordering ingredients and managing inventory to ensure smooth kitchen operations.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate menu forecasting leading to stockouts or overstocking
  • ▸ Vendor reliability issues affecting timely deliveries
  • ▸ Quality control problems with received goods
  • ▸ Data entry errors in Oracle OPERA Cloud causing inventory discrepancies
  • ▸ Difficulty in managing multiple vendors and contracts
