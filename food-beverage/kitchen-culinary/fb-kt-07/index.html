ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FB-KT-07
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Kitchen & Culinary Operations
  • Process Allergen Management & Labelling
  • Trigger A guest requests an allergen-free meal or a specific allergy warning label on their menu items.
  • Outcome Accurate allergen information is provided to guests, and all relevant menu items are properly labeled. The hotel maintains compliance with local food safety regulations and ensures guest satisfaction.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per guest request
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute for notification to Kitchen Manager
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per allergen information review
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for preparing an allergen-free menu
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per labeled menu item
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per week for continuous improvement

Description

This process ensures proper allergen management and labeling in full-service Marriott hotels to comply with food safety regulations and cater to guests with dietary restrictions.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate allergen data leading to guest dissatisfaction or health risks
  • ▸ Delays in communication between departments affecting service quality
  • ▸ Mislabeling of menu items causing confusion and potential safety issues
  • ▸ Difficulty in managing kitchen workflow during peak times
  • ▸ Failure to continuously improve allergen management processes
