Identification

  • ID FB-KT-03
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Kitchen & Culinary Operations
  • Process Cold Chain & Storage Management
  • Trigger Initiation of a new inventory order for perishable items
  • Outcome Successful monitoring, storage, and tracking of all cold chain products throughout their lifecycle within the hotel.

KPIs

  • ▸ < 24 hours for order creation and confirmation
  • ▸ < 30 minutes for receiving inspection and temperature monitoring
  • ▸ < 1 hour for storage allocation, inventory update, and usage tracking
  • ▸ < 1 day for reorder notification after usage tracking

Description

This process outlines the management of cold chain and storage in a full-service Marriott hotel's kitchen operations to ensure food safety and quality.

Risks

  • ▸ Vendor unreliability or delayed deliveries
  • ▸ Inadequate storage space leading to product spoilage
  • ▸ Equipment malfunction affecting temperature monitoring
  • ▸ Data entry errors in inventory systems
  • ▸ Overuse or waste of perishable items due to improper management
