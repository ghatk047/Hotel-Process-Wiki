Description

Menu Engineering & Recipe Costing process at a Marriott full-service hotel involves developing and costing new menu items to ensure profitability while maintaining quality.

Risks

  • ▸ Ingredient price volatility leading to inaccurate costing
  • ▸ Budget constraints affecting approval of new menu items
  • ▸ Staff resistance to change during training sessions
  • ▸ Sales performance below expectations due to new menu item introduction
  • ▸ System downtime impacting final implementation of optimized menu item
