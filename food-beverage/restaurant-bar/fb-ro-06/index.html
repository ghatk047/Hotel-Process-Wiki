KPIs

  • ▸ Completion time for pre-open preparation less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per shift adjustment in staff scheduling
  • ▸ Inventory check completion within 15 minutes
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to open restaurant and bar
  • ▸ Continuous monitoring with less than 1 minute intervals for dining area status
  • ▸ Completion of post-close cleanup within 30 minutes

Description

Operational process for managing all-day dining operations at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring smooth service and efficient use of resources.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate inventory tracking leading to stockouts or overstocking
  • ▸ Staff shortages during peak hours affecting customer service
  • ▸ Delayed reservations causing guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Difficulty in maintaining cleanliness during high traffic periods
  • ▸ Errors in data analysis leading to incorrect operational insights
