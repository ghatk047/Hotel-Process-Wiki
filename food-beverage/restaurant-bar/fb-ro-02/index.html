Identification

  • ID FB-RO-02
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Restaurant & Bar Operations
  • Process Restaurant Opening & Mise en Place
  • Trigger A request from the Food & Beverage Manager to open a new restaurant or bar, or to reconfigure an existing one.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in a fully operational restaurant with all necessary equipment and supplies ready for service.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 24 hours to approval of restaurant opening request
  • ▸ Within 48 hours of approval for role assignment document
  • ▸ Less than 5 business days for equipment and supply delivery
  • ▸ Within 3 days of equipment delivery for completed restaurant setup
  • ▸ Within 2 days of layout completion for configured POS system
  • ▸ Within 1 week of POS configuration for trained staff list

Description

The process for opening a restaurant and setting up the mise en place at a full-service Marriott hotel involves coordination between various departments to ensure smooth operation.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in obtaining necessary approvals from upper management.
  • ▸ Ensuring all team members are trained and available for the new setup.
  • ▸ Managing inventory levels and ensuring timely delivery.
  • ▸ Coordinating with multiple departments for seamless setup.
  • ▸ Maintaining high training standards and ensuring all staff are competent.
