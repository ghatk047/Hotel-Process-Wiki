KPIs

Description

The process outlines the setup and management of a breakfast buffet at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS. It ensures efficient service delivery, inventory control, and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect inventory levels leading to menu adjustments
  • ▸ Delays in supply delivery affecting service
  • ▸ Inconsistent food quality due to preparation errors
  • ▸ Over/underutilization of buffet resources
  • ▸ Unmet special requests affecting guest satisfaction
