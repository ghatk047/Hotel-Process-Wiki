  • Trigger A guest reservation is made or a walk-in customer arrives for dining or beverages.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in a high-quality dining experience for guests, accurate billing, and effective inventory management.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per reservation entry
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes per table for menu selection

  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per day for inventory review

Description

This process outlines the Food & Beverage Service Standards at a full-service Marriott hotel, focusing on restaurant and bar operations. It ensures consistent quality of service and efficient management of food and beverage offerings.

Risks

  • ▸ High volume of reservations leading to operational delays
  • ▸ Supply chain disruptions affecting food and beverage availability
  • ▸ Guest complaints or dissatisfaction with service quality
  • ▸ Technical issues with Oracle OPERA Cloud or other systems
  • ▸ Inaccurate inventory management leading to stockouts
