Identification

  • ID FB-RO-01
  • Domain Food & Beverage Operations
  • Group Restaurant & Bar Operations
  • Process Table Reservation Management
  • Trigger Customer request for a table reservation through various channels such as phone, online booking platforms, or directly at the hotel.
  • Outcome Successful completion of the reservation process with accurate details recorded in the PMS system, confirmation sent to the customer, and the reservation reflected in the restaurant's schedule.

KPIs

  • ▸ < 3 min response time for reservation requests
  • ▸ < 5 min availability check and schedule update
  • ▸ < 2 min confirmation and notification processes
  • ▸ < 10 min table preparation time
  • ▸ < 5 min feedback collection and record updating

Description

This process manages table reservations at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud and other Marriott systems to ensure efficient and seamless operations.

Risks

  • ▸ System downtime affecting reservation processing
  • ▸ Incorrect information leading to double bookings or missed reservations
  • ▸ Customer dissatisfaction due to poor communication or service delays
  • ▸ Inaccurate feedback recording impacting future guest experiences
  • ▸ Resource allocation issues during peak dining hours
