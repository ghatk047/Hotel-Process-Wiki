Identification

  • ID FN-AC-01
  • Domain Finance & Procurement
  • Group Accounting & Financial Control
  • Process Accounts Payable Processing
  • Trigger Receipt of an invoice from a vendor or automatic generation of an invoice through the Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS system.
  • Outcome Successful completion produces a fully processed and paid invoice, updated financial records in Oracle OPERA Cloud, and accurate reporting for financial control.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per invoice receipt and validation
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per payment processing
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes per financial record update
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour per report generation and review

Description

The Accounts Payable Processing (FN-AC-01) is a critical financial process at a Marriott full-service hotel. It ensures timely and accurate payment of invoices to vendors while maintaining compliance with internal controls.

Risks

  • ▸ Data discrepancies between Oracle OPERA Cloud and SAP S/4HANA
  • ▸ Payment delays due to system integration issues
  • ▸ Audit findings leading to additional payment processing time
  • ▸ Vendor disputes over invoice accuracy
