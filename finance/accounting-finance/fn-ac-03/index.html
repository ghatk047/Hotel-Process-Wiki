Description

The General Ledger Month-End Close process at a Marriott full-service hotel ensures accurate financial reporting by consolidating and reconciling all financial transactions from various systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Data discrepancies between different systems leading to inaccurate financial reporting
  • ▸ Errors in posting adjustments affecting the accuracy of the General Ledger
  • ▸ Delays in generating final reports due to system issues or data errors
