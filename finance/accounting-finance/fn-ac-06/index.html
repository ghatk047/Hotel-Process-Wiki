  • Outcome A comprehensive budget document with variance analysis, enabling informed financial decisions and operational adjustments.

KPIs

  • ▸ Budget preparation plan initiated within 2 days
  • ▸ Revenue and expense forecasts completed within 1 week
  • ▸ Approved budget document reviewed and approved within 1 week
  • ▸ Variance analysis report generated daily
  • ▸ Financial report prepared within 2 weeks
  • ▸ Adjustments implemented within 1 month

Description

The FN-AC-06 process involves preparing the annual budget and analyzing variances against actual financial performance at a Marriott full-service hotel. This ensures financial control and helps in strategic decision-making.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data from Oracle OPERA Cloud leading to flawed forecasts
  • ▸ Difficulty in aligning budget with strategic goals due to market volatility
  • ▸ Real-time data integration challenges affecting variance analysis accuracy
  • ▸ Misalignment between financial performance and strategic objectives
  • ▸ Effective communication breakdown during implementation of adjustments
