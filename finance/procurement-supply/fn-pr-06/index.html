  • Trigger Low stock alerts from Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS
  • Outcome Inventory is replenished, PAR (Par Level) is updated, and all systems are synchronized with accurate stock data.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes to identify low stock items
  • ▸ Less than 3 minutes to generate the list
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to review and approve
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes to receive and record inventory

Description

This process ensures that inventory levels are maintained at optimal levels to meet guest needs and operational efficiency in a full-service Marriott hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Supplier communication delays can cause stockouts.
  • ▸ Delayed updates from suppliers can disrupt inventory management.
  • ▸ Manual data entry can lead to errors.
