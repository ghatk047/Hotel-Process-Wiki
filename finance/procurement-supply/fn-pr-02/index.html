Identification

  • ID FN-PR-02
  • Domain Finance & Procurement
  • Group Procurement & Supply Chain
  • Process Supplier Onboarding & Qualification
  • Trigger Initiation by the Procurement Manager upon identification of a new supplier or existing supplier needing re-evaluation.
  • Outcome A fully qualified supplier with approved contracts and documented compliance with Marriott's procurement policies and procedures.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 1 day to initiate request
  • ▸ Less than 3 days to review information
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours for the interview
  • ▸ Less than 5 days to receive documentation
  • ▸ Less than 3 days to verify compliance
  • ▸ Less than 1 week to negotiate terms

Description

The process of onboarding and qualifying suppliers to ensure they meet Marriott International's standards for quality, reliability, and cost-effectiveness in a full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Delays in initiating the onboarding process due to incomplete information.
  • ▸ Non-compliance with requested documentation.
  • ▸ Difficulties in reaching agreement on contract terms.
  • ▸ Legal review delays due to complex contract language.
  • ▸ Data entry errors leading to incorrect supplier information.
