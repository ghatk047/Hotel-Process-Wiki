ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FN-PR-01
  • Domain Finance & Procurement
  • Group Procurement & Supply Chain
  • Process Purchase Requisition & Approval
  • Trigger A department head or manager identifies the need for new supplies, equipment, or services.
  • Outcome A successfully approved purchase requisition that leads to the procurement of required items or services.

KPIs

  • ▸ Purchase requisition initiation within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Review and approval of purchase requisition within 15 minutes
  • ▸ Generation and sending of purchase order within 20 minutes
  • ▸ Receiving goods/services within 24 hours
  • ▸ Inventory update within 30 minutes

Description

The FN-PR-01 process ensures that purchase requisitions are properly initiated, reviewed, and approved within a Marriott full-service hotel. This process is crucial for managing procurement activities efficiently and adhering to financial controls.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect item codes or quantities leading to delays in procurement.
  • ▸ Overlooking budget constraints during the review process.
  • ▸ Delays in approving purchase requisitions due to financial concerns.
  • ▸ Supplier communication issues causing delays in delivery.
  • ▸ Inventory discrepancies due to manual entry errors.
