ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID FN-PR-08
  • Domain Finance & Procurement
  • Group Procurement & Supply Chain
  • Process Supplier Performance Review
  • Trigger Quarterly or upon significant changes in supplier performance or contract terms
  • Outcome A comprehensive report detailing the performance of suppliers, including strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT), along with actionable recommendations for improvement. This leads to informed decision-making regarding supplier contracts and relationships.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data gathering and feedback collection within 2 days
  • ▸ SWOT analysis completed in less than 2 days
  • ▸ Recommendations developed in less than 1 day
  • ▸ Supplier interviews conducted within 1 week
  • ▸ Stakeholder communication plan finalized in less than 1 day

Description

The Supplier Performance Review process is a structured evaluation of suppliers' performance to ensure they meet Marriott Bonvoy's standards and expectations in terms of quality, cost, delivery, and service. This review helps in maintaining high levels of guest satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Incomplete or inaccurate data collection leading to flawed analysis
  • ▸ Difficulty in coordinating schedules for supplier interviews
  • ▸ Resistance from suppliers during the review process
  • ▸ Challenges in aligning recommendations with business strategy and budget constraints
  • ▸ Inconsistent implementation of improvements across different departments
