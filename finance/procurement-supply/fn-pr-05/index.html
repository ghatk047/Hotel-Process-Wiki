  • ID FN-PR-05
  • Domain Finance & Procurement
  • Group Procurement & Supply Chain
  • Process OS&E Procurement
  • Trigger Initiated by the Procurement Manager upon identification of OS&E needs based on inventory levels, usage reports, or upcoming events.
  • Outcome Successful completion results in the procurement of required OS&E items at optimal prices, ensuring minimal downtime and maintaining high operational standards.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 hours for identifying OS&E needs
  • ▸ Less than 1 day for creating procurement plans
  • ▸ Less than 1 day for placing purchase orders
  • ▸ Less than 1 day for updating inventory

Description

The FN-PR-05 process is responsible for procurement of operating supplies and equipment (OS&E) at a Marriott full-service hotel. This ensures that the hotel maintains its operational efficiency and guest satisfaction through timely and cost-effective acquisition of necessary items.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate forecasting of OS&E needs leading to stockouts or overstocking.
  • ▸ Supplier selection based on incomplete or inaccurate information.
  • ▸ Delays in order processing and delivery affecting hotel operations.
