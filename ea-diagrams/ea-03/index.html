Revenue & Distribution Architecture

✅ Published Ref: Marriott International Revenue management stack — demand signals to rate decisions to channel push
🗺️ Enterprise Architecture Diagram Click to open fullscreen · Scroll to zoom · Drag to pan · Double-click to reset
📋 Architecture Notes

This diagram shows Marriott's revenue management and distribution architecture. IDeaS G3 RMS ingests demand signals from OPERA PMS, competitive intelligence from RateGain, and forward-looking data from the Bonvoy CRM to produce optimised rate recommendations. Approved rates push to SynXis CRS which distributes to all channels: OTAs via channel manager, GDS via Sabre/Amadeus connectivity, and Marriott.com direct. Rate parity monitoring runs continuously and feeds alerts back to the revenue management team via Power BI dashboards.
