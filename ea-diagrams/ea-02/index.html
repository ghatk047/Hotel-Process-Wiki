Guest Journey Data Flow

✅ Published Ref: Marriott International How guest data flows from booking through arrival, stay, checkout, and post-stay loyalty
🗺️ Enterprise Architecture Diagram Click to open fullscreen · Scroll to zoom · Drag to pan · Double-click to reset
📋 Architecture Notes

This diagram traces the complete Marriott guest data journey across all systems. A reservation originates in an OTA or direct channel, passes through SynXis CRS into OPERA Cloud PMS, and triggers pre-arrival workflows in the Bonvoy App. At check-in, OPERA orchestrates room assignment, digital key issuance via VingCard, and INNCOM room controls. During the stay, all charges from MICROS POS, spa, and ancillaries post to the guest folio. At checkout, SAP captures revenue and Bonvoy CRM posts points. Post-stay survey data feeds back to Salesforce for account management and NPS tracking.
