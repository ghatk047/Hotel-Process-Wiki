Loyalty & CRM Architecture

✅ Published Ref: Marriott International Marriott Bonvoy platform — enrolment, points, elite status, and guest personalisation
🗺️ Enterprise Architecture Diagram Click to open fullscreen · Scroll to zoom · Drag to pan · Double-click to reset
📋 Architecture Notes

This diagram maps the Marriott Bonvoy loyalty and CRM technology ecosystem. The Bonvoy Platform is the central loyalty engine, managing 196 million member profiles, points accrual rules, elite tier calculations, and award inventory. OPERA Cloud PMS feeds stay data and charges to Bonvoy for points posting. Salesforce CRM manages corporate accounts, group sales, and TMC relationships. The Bonvoy App serves as the primary member touchpoint for booking, digital key, chat, and points management. Post-stay survey data from Qualtrics feeds NPS scores back to Salesforce for account health tracking and service recovery.
