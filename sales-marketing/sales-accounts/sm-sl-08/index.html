ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID SM-SL-08
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Sales & Account Management
  • Process Consortia & Preferred Programme Management
  • Trigger Receiving a request for a new consortium or preferred programme account from the Sales team.
  • Outcome Successful onboarding of a new consortium or preferred programme account with established communication channels and integrated systems.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for account request initiation
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes to assign an Account Manager
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for consortium/preferred programme review
  • ▸ Daily campaign performance monitoring within 30 minutes

Description

This process manages consortia and preferred programme accounts at a Marriott full-service hotel, ensuring seamless coordination between sales teams, marketing efforts, and loyalty programs.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate financial assessment leading to poor decision-making
  • ▸ Marketing campaigns failing to reach the target audience effectively
  • ▸ Balancing rewards in loyalty programs without impacting profitability
  • ▸ Stakeholders not being fully informed or onboarded
  • ▸ Missing final steps during account onboarding
