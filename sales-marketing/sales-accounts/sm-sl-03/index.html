KPIs

  • ▸ < 5 min response time for initial contact

Description

Process for managing MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) sales at a Marriott full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate lead qualification leading to missed sales opportunities
  • ▸ Resource allocation delays affecting event timeline
  • ▸ Unexpected issues during event execution impacting guest satisfaction
  • ▸ Poor feedback handling and implementation of improvement actions
  • ▸ Client dissatisfaction due to inadequate follow-up post-event
