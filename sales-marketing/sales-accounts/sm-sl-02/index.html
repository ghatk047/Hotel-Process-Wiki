Identification

  • ID SM-SL-02
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Sales & Account Management
  • Process Contract Negotiation & Rate Agreement
  • Trigger Receiving a request for proposal (RFP) from a potential client or identifying a new business opportunity.
  • Outcome Successful negotiation and signing of a rate agreement that aligns with the hotel's revenue goals and client expectations.

KPIs

  • ▸ Time to initiate negotiation < 24 hours
  • ▸ Proposal preparation time < 48 hours
  • ▸ Number of negotiation rounds < 3
  • ▸ Time to finalize and sign < 7 days
  • ▸ Time to update contract database < 24 hours
  • ▸ Weekly performance review < 4 hours

Description

The process of negotiating and finalizing contract agreements with clients at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Client rejecting the proposal due to unfavorable terms
  • ▸ Legal delays in finalizing the agreement
  • ▸ Data integrity issues during contract updates
  • ▸ Inaccurate data reporting leading to wrong decisions
  • ▸ Client dissatisfaction affecting future business opportunities
