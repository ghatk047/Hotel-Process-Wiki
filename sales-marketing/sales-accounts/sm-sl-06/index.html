Identification

  • ID SM-SL-06
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Sales & Account Management
  • Process Key Account Review & Retention
  • Trigger Quarterly review of key accounts identified by the Revenue Management System (RMS) as high-value or at risk of churn.
  • Outcome Successful completion ensures that key accounts are reviewed, their needs are understood, and retention strategies are implemented to maintain long-term relationships with these customers.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 1 hour to generate list of key accounts
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes to schedule meetings
  • ▸ Less than 60 minutes per meeting for initial and follow-up reviews
  • ▸ Less than 2 hours to complete data analysis
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour to develop retention strategy
  • ▸ Less than 1 hour to adjust retention strategy

Description

A structured process document for SM-SL-06 Key Account Review & Retention at a Marriott full-service hotel, focusing on sales and account management.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate identification of key accounts due to complex criteria
  • ▸ Conflicting schedules and availability for meeting scheduling
  • ▸ Handling multiple accounts simultaneously during review meetings
  • ▸ Complex data interpretation leading to inaccurate analysis
  • ▸ Coordination across multiple departments for implementing retention actions
