Identification

  • ID SM-MK-03
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Marketing & Digital
  • Process SEO & SEM Campaign Management
  • Trigger The need to enhance online visibility and drive bookings through search engine optimization (SEO) and search engine marketing (SEM) efforts.
  • Outcome Successful completion of the campaign results in increased website traffic, higher conversion rates, and improved hotel occupancy rates.

KPIs

  • ▸ Campaign setup time less than 24 hours
  • ▸ Daily campaign optimization review in under 30 minutes
  • ▸ Weekly performance analysis report completed in less than 1 hour
  • ▸ SEO content creation time less than 8 hours per page
  • ▸ SEM campaign ROI improvement by at least 15%

Description

A structured process document for managing SEO & SEM campaigns at a Marriott full-service hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key Marriott systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate keyword research leading to low-performing campaigns
  • ▸ Content quality issues affecting SEO rankings
  • ▸ Budget overruns due to unexpected SEM costs
  • ▸ Lack of alignment between marketing strategy and business goals
  • ▸ Data security risks from improper campaign data archiving
