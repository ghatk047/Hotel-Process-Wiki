Identification

  • ID SM-MK-06
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty

  • Process Photography & Content Production
  • Trigger Initiation of a new marketing campaign or seasonal event requiring visual content
  • Outcome Successful completion produces a set of high-quality photographs and associated content that align with the campaign objectives and are ready for distribution across various digital platforms.

KPIs

  • ▸ Conceptualize Content Plan: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Photograph Capture: Less than 4 hours per shoot
  • ▸ Content Creation: Less than 10 hours
  • ▸ Content Review and Approval: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Content Distribution: Less than 1 hour
  • ▸ Performance Monitoring: Less than 2 hours daily

Description

A structured process document for photography and content production at a Marriott full-service hotel, focusing on capturing high-quality images to support marketing campaigns and digital initiatives.

Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent brand standards during content creation
  • ▸ Delays in photographer availability affecting shoot schedules
  • ▸ Technical issues with social media platforms impacting distribution
  • ▸ Poor engagement rates despite timely and relevant content
  • ▸ Data inaccuracies leading to misinformed decision-making
