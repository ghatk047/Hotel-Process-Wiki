Risks

  • ▸ Inconsistent brand voice and quality in content creation
  • ▸ Difficulty optimizing posting times for maximum engagement
  • ▸ Challenges in summarizing complex data into clear actionable reports
  • ▸ Strategic misalignment between social media initiatives and business goals
  • ▸ Technical issues with social media management tools
