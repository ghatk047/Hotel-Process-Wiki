  • Group Franchise & Brand Management
  • Process Franchise Renewal & Exit Management
  • Trigger Initiation by the Franchise Manager upon contract expiration or early termination notice
  • Outcome Successful completion results in a renewed franchise agreement with updated terms or a smooth exit process that minimizes disruption to hotel operations.

KPIs

  • ▸ Renewal or exit strategy document prepared within less than 1 week
  • ▸ Financial projection report completed within less than 1 week
  • ▸ Market analysis report ready within less than 3 weeks
  • ▸ Renewal agreement approved within less than 2 weeks

Description

The SM-FR-07 process manages the renewal and exit of franchise agreements at Marriott full-service hotels. This ensures seamless transitions between contract periods, maintaining brand standards and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Complex contract negotiations leading to delays
  • ▸ Inaccurate financial forecasting affecting renewal terms
  • ▸ Difficulty in maintaining open communication with franchisees
  • ▸ Challenges in managing staff transitions and asset handovers during exit
  • ▸ Failure to identify key lessons from the exit process for future improvements
