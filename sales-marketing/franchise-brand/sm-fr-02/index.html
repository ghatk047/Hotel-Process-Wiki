KPIs

  • ▸ Audit plan creation within 2 hours
  • ▸ Sales audit completion per team member in less than 3 hours
  • ▸ Marketing audit completion per team member in less than 4 hours

Description

The SM-FR-02 Brand Standard Audit & Quality Assurance process ensures that Marriott Bonvoy franchise hotels meet brand standards in sales, marketing, and loyalty programs. This audit helps maintain high-quality guest experiences and operational efficiency.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data collection from multiple systems
  • ▸ Overlooking critical areas during the audit process
  • ▸ Difficulty in integrating data from various platforms
  • ▸ Resistance to change among hotel staff
  • ▸ Delayed implementation of action plan tasks
