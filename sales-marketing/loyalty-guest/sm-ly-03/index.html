ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID SM-LY-03
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Loyalty & Guest Relationship
  • Process Points Redemption & Award Fulfilment
  • Trigger A guest initiates the redemption of loyalty points through the Marriott Bonvoy app, website, or directly with a hotel staff member.
  • Outcome The guest receives the requested award (e.g., room upgrade, dining credit) in exchange for their loyalty points.

KPIs

  • ▸ Redemption request logged in Oracle OPERA Cloud within 2 minutes
  • ▸ Eligibility verification completed within 5 minutes
  • ▸ Award prepared and fulfilled within 7 minutes
  • ▸ Loyalty program records updated within 1 minute

Description

This process outlines how Marriott Bonvoy loyalty points are redeemed and awards are fulfilled at a full-service Marriott hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Incorrect redemption eligibility verification leading to guest dissatisfaction
  • ▸ Inventory mismanagement causing overbooking or underutilization
  • ▸ Communication delays between systems resulting in award fulfillment issues
  • ▸ Data entry errors affecting loyalty point allocation and tracking
  • ▸ System downtime impacting the speed of redemption processing
