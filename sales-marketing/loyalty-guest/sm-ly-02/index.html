KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes per transaction for loyalty status identification and points calculation
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute per transaction for posting points to the loyalty system
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute per transaction for generating and sending loyalty statements
  • ▸ Less than 30 minutes per day for monitoring points accrual and posting

Description

Process for managing points accrual and posting in Marriott Bonvoy loyalty program at a full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Inaccurate data entry leading to incorrect points accrual
  • ▸ System connectivity issues affecting points posting
  • ▸ Manual verification errors resulting in inaccurate loyalty statements
  • ▸ Email delivery failures causing missed communication opportunities
  • ▸ Outdated guest profiles impacting upselling opportunities
