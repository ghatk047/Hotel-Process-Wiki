Description

The process involves collecting post-stay feedback from guests to improve service quality and manage Net Promoter Score (NPS) at a Marriott full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ Low survey response rates leading to inaccurate data
  • ▸ Inconsistent data entry causing errors in analysis
  • ▸ Difficulty in coordinating cross-departmental efforts for improvements
  • ▸ Challenges in measuring the long-term impact of implemented changes
  • ▸ Communication barriers when reporting complex data insights to senior management
