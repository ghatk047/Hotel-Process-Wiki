ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID SM-LY-07
  • Domain Sales, Marketing & Loyalty
  • Group Loyalty & Guest Relationship
  • Process Anniversary & Special Occasion Management
  • Trigger Anniversary or special occasion event is scheduled by the hotel or requested by a guest through the Marriott Bonvoy CRM system.
  • Outcome Successful execution of the anniversary or special occasion event with high guest satisfaction, optimized resource allocation, and accurate tracking of all related activities and expenses.

KPIs

  • ▸ Event request processing time: Less than 5 minutes
  • ▸ Room availability confirmation time: Less than 15 minutes
  • ▸ Catering plan finalization time: Less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Transportation arrangements time: Less than 20 minutes
  • ▸ Event execution disruption rate: Less than 5%
  • ▸ Post-event review completion time: Less than 1 hour

Description

This process outlines how a Marriott full-service hotel manages anniversary and special occasion events, ensuring seamless coordination between departments and systems to enhance guest experiences.

Risks

  • ▸ Conflicting event requests leading to room allocation issues
  • ▸ Menu adjustments for special dietary needs causing delays
  • ▸ Unforeseen travel disruptions affecting transportation arrangements
  • ▸ On-site emergencies during event execution
  • ▸ Data accuracy errors in guest records update
