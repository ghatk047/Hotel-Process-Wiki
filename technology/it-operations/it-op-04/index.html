ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID IT-OP-04
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group IT Operations & Infrastructure
  • Process Guest Technology Support
  • Trigger Guest initiates a request for technology support through various channels such as phone, email, or in-person at the front desk.
  • Outcome The issue is resolved to the satisfaction of the guest, with minimal disruption to their stay.

KPIs

  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for receiving and logging a guest request
  • ▸ Less than 1 minute for assigning a ticket to a specialist
  • ▸ Less than 5 minutes for diagnosing an issue
  • ▸ Less than 10 minutes for resolving an issue
  • ▸ Less than 2 minutes for verifying resolution with the guest

Description

This process outlines the steps involved in providing guest technology support at a full-service Marriott hotel using Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS and other key systems.

Risks

  • ▸ Overlooking critical issues due to high volume of requests
  • ▸ Inaccurate diagnosis leading to prolonged resolution times
  • ▸ Difficulty in maintaining up-to-date knowledge base articles
  • ▸ Resource allocation challenges during peak periods
  • ▸ Guest dissatisfaction due to unresolved or delayed issues
