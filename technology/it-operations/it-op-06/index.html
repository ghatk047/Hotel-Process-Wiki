ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID IT-OP-06
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group IT Operations & Infrastructure
  • Process Data Backup & Business Continuity
  • Trigger Daily automated trigger by IT Operations team
  • Outcome Successful completion produces complete, secure backups of all key Marriott systems and updates the Business Continuity Plan (BCP).

KPIs

  • ▸ Backup completion within 10 min of initiation
  • ▸ BCP update accuracy rate >95%
  • ▸ Restoration success rate >98%
  • ▸ Audit report generation time <30 min

Description

This process ensures regular data backup and business continuity at a Marriott full-service hotel to protect critical systems and data.

Risks

  • ▸ Data corruption during backup process
  • ▸ Inadequate testing leading to failed restorations
  • ▸ Outdated BCPs causing operational disruptions in emergencies
  • ▸ Insufficient resources for implementing improvement actions
  • ▸ Continuous monitoring and adjustments becoming too resource-intensive
