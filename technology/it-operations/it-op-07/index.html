Identification

  • ID IT-OP-07
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group IT Operations & Infrastructure
  • Process Software Licence & Vendor Management
  • Trigger Annual review of software licenses and quarterly updates to vendor management.
  • Outcome A comprehensive inventory of active software licenses and vendors, with updated contracts and renewals scheduled.

KPIs

  • ▸ License review completed within 2 hours
  • ▸ Vendor audits conducted within 2 weeks
  • ▸ Renewal notifications prepared within 1 week
  • ▸ Finalized contracts signed within 4 weeks

Description

This process manages software licenses and vendor agreements for Marriott Bonvoy hotels, ensuring compliance with contractual obligations and optimizing system performance.

Risks

  • ▸ Non-compliance with software license agreements leading to legal action.
  • ▸ Delays in vendor renewals causing system downtime.
  • ▸ Inaccurate internal documentation affecting operational decisions.
