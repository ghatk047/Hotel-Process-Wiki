ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID IT-OP-02
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group IT Operations & Infrastructure
  • Process Network & WiFi Infrastructure Management
  • Trigger Weekly maintenance schedule or upon detection of network issues
  • Outcome A stable, high-speed network environment with reliable WiFi connectivity for all hotel operations.

KPIs

  • ▸ Network audit completion time: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Maintenance plan creation time: Less than 30 minutes
  • ▸ Firmware/software update time: Less than 4 hours
  • ▸ Network configuration time: Less than 3 hours
  • ▸ Connectivity test time: Less than 2 hours
  • ▸ Change management document review time: Less than 1 hour

Description

Manage and maintain the network and WiFi infrastructure at a Marriott full-service hotel to ensure seamless connectivity for guests and staff.

Risks

  • ▸ Inadequate network auditing leading to undetected issues
  • ▸ Incorrect firmware/software updates causing system instability
  • ▸ Misconfigured network settings resulting in connectivity problems
  • ▸ Outdated WiFi credentials exposing security risks
  • ▸ Lack of proper documentation and review processes leading to unresolved issues
