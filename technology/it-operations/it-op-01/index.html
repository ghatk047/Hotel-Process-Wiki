Identification

  • ID IT-OP-01
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group IT Operations & Infrastructure
  • Process PMS Administration
  • Trigger Scheduled maintenance window or system alert
  • Outcome Successful completion of PMS administration tasks, ensuring the hotel's reservation and guest management systems are up-to-date and operational.

KPIs

  • ▸ Database backup completion within 30 minutes
  • ▸ Software update completion within 60 minutes
  • ▸ Less than 5 issues identified per day in Marriott Bonvoy CRM
  • ▸ Completion of financial reports within 2 hours
  • ▸ Completion of HR changes approval within 1 hour per request
  • ▸ Functional and accurate dashboards within 1 hour

Description

The IT-OP-01 process involves managing and maintaining Oracle OPERA Cloud PMS at a Marriott full-service hotel. This ensures seamless operations, data accuracy, and guest satisfaction.

Risks

  • ▸ Inadequate storage space for database backups
  • ▸ Compatibility issues between systems during updates
  • ▸ Data mismatch between CRS and PMS
  • ▸ Vendor contract discrepancies affecting procurement
  • ▸ Delays in processing HR requests due to communication gaps
