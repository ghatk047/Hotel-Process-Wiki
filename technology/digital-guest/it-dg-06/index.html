ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID IT-DG-06
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group Digital Guest Experience
  • Process Guest Feedback Platform Management
  • Trigger Guest submits feedback via digital channels (e.g., mobile app, website, in-room tablet)
  • Outcome Effective handling of guest feedback leading to improved guest satisfaction and operational improvements.

KPIs

  • ▸ < 3 min response time for feedback collection
  • ▸ < 10 min per feedback review
  • ▸ < 1 day for minor issues, < 3 days for major issues in action plan execution
  • ▸ < 2 min per feedback response
  • ▸ < 1 day per week for trend analysis
  • ▸ < 1 week per improvement implementation

Description

This process outlines the management of a guest feedback platform at a full-service Marriott hotel, ensuring timely collection, analysis, and response to guest feedback.

Risks

  • ▸ High volume of feedback leading to delays in processing
  • ▸ Inaccurate categorization or assignment of feedback
  • ▸ Resistance from departments to implement changes based on feedback
  • ▸ Difficulty in personalizing responses to each guest
  • ▸ Challenges in aligning departmental efforts with hotel-wide goals
