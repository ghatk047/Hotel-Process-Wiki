ℹ️ Process Attributes

Identification

  • ID IT-DG-01
  • Domain Technology & Systems
  • Group Digital Guest Experience
  • Process Guest App Management
  • Trigger A new guest app feature is requested by the Digital Experience team or an update is needed based on feedback from guests or system maintenance.
  • Outcome Successful implementation of the guest app feature or update, ensuring seamless operation across all relevant systems and a positive guest experience.

KPIs

  • ▸ Feature analysis report within 24 hours
  • ▸ System impact assessment report within 48 hours
  • ▸ Feature development completed within 3 weeks
  • ▸ Post-deployment monitoring for 24 hours

Description

Manage guest app functionalities and updates at a Marriott full-service hotel to enhance digital guest experience.

Risks

  • ▸ Incompatibility issues with existing systems
  • ▸ Deployment disruptions affecting guest app availability
  • ▸ High volume of bugs during testing and quality assurance
  • ▸ Resource constraints impacting timely feature development
  • ▸ Negative guest feedback leading to further optimization needs
