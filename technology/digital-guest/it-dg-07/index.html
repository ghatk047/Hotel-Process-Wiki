Identification

  • Group Digital Guest Experience
  • Process CRM Data Quality & Deduplication
  • Trigger Monthly review of CRM data quality metrics identified through Knowcross HotSOS system alerts.
  • Outcome A clean, accurate, and deduplicated CRM database that supports targeted marketing campaigns and personalized guest experiences.

KPIs

  • ▸ Data extraction within 2 hours
  • ▸ Data integration within 1 hour
  • ▸ Data deduplication within 30 minutes
  • ▸ CRM data validation completion within 2 hours
  • ▸ Data enrichment within 1 hour
  • ▸ Data deployment within 2 hours

Description

Ensures high-quality and deduplicated CRM data to enhance guest experience and operational efficiency at a Marriott full-service hotel.

Risks

  • ▸ System compatibility issues during data extraction and upload
  • ▸ Manual validation errors affecting data accuracy
  • ▸ Disagreements on data quality metrics among executive team
  • ▸ False positives in monitoring alerts leading to unnecessary interventions
  • ▸ Inaccurate booking data impacting enrichment processes
