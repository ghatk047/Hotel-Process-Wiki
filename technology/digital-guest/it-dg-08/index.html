KPIs

  • ▸ Data integration plan completion within 1 week
  • ▸ BI dashboard design and development within 9 days

  • ▸ Training completion rate above 80%
  • ▸ Daily monitoring of BI dashboard performance with less than 5% downtime

Description

The IT-DG-08 process involves setting up and maintaining data analytics and BI reporting systems to enhance the digital guest experience at a Marriott full-service hotel. This includes integrating various Marriott systems to provide comprehensive insights for decision-making.

Risks

  • ▸ Incompatibility issues between Marriott systems
  • ▸ Data accuracy and synchronization problems
  • ▸ Complexity in developing data models that meet business needs
  • ▸ Technical challenges during the deployment phase
  • ▸ Resistance to change among hotel staff regarding new BI tools
